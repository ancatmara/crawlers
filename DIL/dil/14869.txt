      <p class="result"> 
 <span class="edilb">C</span> Oir. dech mad + verb = dech + verb (A e supra), 
<span style="color: #ff8000">superlative</span> of mad + verb (mad-bocht, mad-búi, mad-génair 
<span class="lbl">&amp;c.</span>). This idiom has been concluded from the following 
instances: 
          caite téchta prímgeine...cach fermac arosloice 
broind a máthar iar cétmuintir cóir cona coibsenaib réir 
a nanmcharat dech mo lesaigther eclais, 
            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Laws" title="eDIL Bibliography">Laws</strong></span> iii 38. 10
          
        
(Senchus Már, main text).

.i. as dech lesaiges eclais, 
            <span class="lbl">ib.</span> 38. 22
          
         (Comm.).—
          Ragallach 
mac Úatach gaeta dé muin geleich: Muirenn deich morochich (.i. rocaín) Cathal deich modereich, 
            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Tig." title="eDIL Bibliography">Tig.</strong></span> A.D. 648

          is tri ḟír flatha cach tlachta

          génair,
