    <p class="result">Mid. Ir. form of <span class="ref"><a class="normal" href="search?search_in=headword&amp;q=namm&#xE1;">nammá</a></span>, 
        <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/PedersenVgl.Gr." title="eDIL Bibliography">Pedersen Vgl. Gr.</strong></span> i 165
      ; of <span class="ref"><a class="normal" href="search?search_in=headword&amp;q=amein">amein</a></span>, Vendr. 
Lex. In Mid. Ir. with unlen. -m(m)-: 
        ammain, 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Mon.Tall." title="eDIL Bibliography">Mon. Tall.</strong></span> 
136.8
        
      . 
        <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/LU" title="eDIL Bibliography">LU</strong></span> 2485
      . 
        <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/TBC-LL1" title="eDIL Bibliography">TBC-LL¹</strong></span> 449
      .

          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Mon.Tall." title="eDIL Bibliography">Mon. Tall.</strong></span> 127.17
        
      . 

        immáin, 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/BColm." title="eDIL Bibliography">BColm.</strong></span> 60.12
        
      . Note also in 
        amáin, 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/TBC-LL1" title="eDIL Bibliography">TBC-LL¹</strong></span> 544

Stressed on second syll., and in classical verse a-bháin, 
a-mháin alliterating with b(h)- and (less frequently) m(h)-. 
Note also aman: 
        dál,
