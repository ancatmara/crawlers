<div class="row result"><div class="col-sm-3 result_headword"><h3 headword_id="31445">maithid</h3><span class="citation">Cite this: eDIL s.v. maithid or <a class="reference" href="31445">dil.ie/31445</a></span><br/></div><div class="col-sm-9 result_content">
   


    <p class="result">
      <span class="pos v" style="color: #ff8000">v</span> 
      <span class="itype i">i</span>. <span class="trans">The orig. meaning may be <span class="def">grants</span>, <span class="def">concedes</span>
      </span>; <span class="lbl">cf.</span> 
        <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Sg." title="eDIL Bibliography">Sg.</strong></span> 
28b5
      : a folad issed maithess in coitchennas <span class="frn ">in nomine</span>
      , gl. 
appellativum commune est multorum quos eadem substantia 
iungit (`<span class="quote">this is what abates</span> (?) <span class="ediltr">the commonness in the noun</span>', 
Thes.; `<span class="quote">was gestattet</span>' 
        <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/PedersenVgl.Gr." title="eDIL Bibliography">Pedersen Vgl. Gr.</strong></span> ii 575
      , <span class="lbl">cf.</span> 
        <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Ascoli" title="eDIL Bibliography">Ascoli</strong></span> ccclxxv
      ), and 
        <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Aen." title="eDIL Bibliography">Aen.</strong></span> 
460
      : mathmait a anum do = <span class="frn ">vitam damus</span>, 
        <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Aeneid" title="eDIL Bibliography">Aeneid</strong></span> ii 145
       ; 
this sense is implicit in many exx. under (<span class="ref"><a class="normal" href="search?search_in=headword&amp;q=maithid (a)">a</a></span>).</p>
  
  
     


      <p class="result"> 
(<span class="edilb">a</span>) <span class="trans">
          <span class="def">remits</span>, <span class="def">excuses</span>
        </span> (an obligation, penalty, <span class="lbl">etc.</span>), with DO of 
person. mathid-som salmu aurnaigti do chetal <span class="ediltr">he excuses the 
singing of the psalms of prayer</span>, 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Mon.Tall." title="eDIL Bibliography">Mon. Tall.</strong></span> 30.
         
          ro maith ind riġ 
a chis do Patraic, 
            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Lat.Lives" title="eDIL Bibliography">Lat. Lives</strong></span> 49.
          
         in maithenn se ni dona 
comadhaib? <span class="ediltr">does he let (us) off any of the conditions ?</span> 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/ZCP" title="eDIL Bibliography">ZCP</strong></span> viii 
549.1
        . Finachta . . . ro maith mor don naem, | tri cóecait cét 
bó, <span class="lbl">etc.</span>, 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/RC" title="eDIL Bibliography">RC</strong></span> xvii 214.3
         = 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Keat." title="eDIL Bibliography">Keat.</strong></span> iii 2242.
         noco ro maith biad 
. . . don cele <span class="ediltr">excused the tenant from food-dues</span>, 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Laws" title="eDIL Bibliography">Laws</strong></span> ii 224.26 
Comm.
         
          maithbet a tinnscra na hingine duib, 
            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/ML" title="eDIL Bibliography">ML</strong></span> 160.15
          
        . ní 
mhaithfidh sé bonn do chlann Luterius <span class="ediltr">will not yield a jot to 
Luther's people</span>, 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Hard." title="eDIL Bibliography">Hard.</strong></span> i 34.
        
      </p>
    
  
  
    
      <p class="result">

          ni ro mathius a olc do

          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Acall." title="eDIL Bibliography">Acall.</strong></span> 1431.

            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Cog." title="eDIL Bibliography">Cog.</strong></span> 104.9
          
        . 
          mataidh-si [maithid <span class="lbl">v.l.</span>] damsa in

            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/CCath." title="eDIL Bibliography">CCath.</strong></span> 5129
          
         (= <span class="frn ">veniam date bella trahenti</span>, 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Luc.Phars." title="eDIL Bibliography">Luc. Phars.</strong></span> vii 
295
        ). 
          maith dhamsa mo fhreagra ort!, 
            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Content." title="eDIL Bibliography">Content.</strong></span> xiv 70.
          
         With

anxious state</span>, 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/A.&#xD3;D&#xE1;laigh" title="eDIL Bibliography">A. Ó Dálaigh</strong></span> lii 1.

          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Luc.Fid." title="eDIL Bibliography">Luc. Fid.</strong></span> 65.7
