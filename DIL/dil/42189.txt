<div class="row result"><div class="col-sm-3 result_headword"><h3 headword_id="42189">túaithbel</h3><span class="citation">Cite this: eDIL s.v. túaithbel or <a class="reference" href="42189">dil.ie/42189</a></span><br/><span class="revision">Last Revised:
                2019</span><br/><p style="margin-top: 4px" class="small">Forms:
                túathal, tuaithbil, tuathbi[u]l, thuathuil</p></div><div class="col-sm-9 result_content">
   
    <p class="result">
      <span class="pos n" style="color: #ff8000">n</span> 
      <span class="itype o">o</span>, <span class="gen m.">m.</span> (túath + sel). In Mod. Ir. also túathal.</p>
   
    
      <p class="result"> 
(<span class="edilb">a</span>) <span class="trans">a <span class="def">turning</span> <span class="def">lefthandwise</span>, <span class="def">against the sun</span>, <span class="def">withershins</span>:</span> 

          
            <span class="frn ">a laeua</span> .i. ō tuaithbel, 
            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/OMulc." title="eDIL Bibliography">O'Mulc.</strong></span> 774
          
        . 
          is blicht la mnāi cur 
ar is donaite cach tuat[h]bel, 
            754
          
        . 
          inní ropu tuathbel 
dosum (Longinus) issed ón robo dess do Críst, 
            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/LB" title="eDIL Bibliography">LB</strong></span> 251a70
          
         
(see under <span class="ref"><a class="normal" href="search?search_in=headword&amp;q=t&#xFA;aisre">túaisre</a></span>). gabdais for imescargain a cheli ic 
malairt mong ┐ ic cirtlam ┐ ic atlugadh tuaithbil
         `<span class="quote">rendant 
coup pour coup</span>,' 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/RC" title="eDIL Bibliography">RC</strong></span> xliii 28.8
         (perh. <span class="pos adverbial" style="color: #ff8000">adverbial</span> here, see (<span class="edilb">b</span>)).</p>
    
  
  
    
      <p class="result"> 
(<span class="edilb">b</span>) As <span class="pos adv." style="color: #ff8000">adv.</span> (<span class="case d">d</span>
        <span class="number s.">s.</span> ?) <span class="trans">
          <span class="def">lefthandwise</span>, <span class="def">from left to right</span>, <span class="def">withershins</span>:</span> 
          desiul scēo tuathbi[u]l do-féce, 
            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/ZCP" title="eDIL Bibliography">ZCP</strong></span> xi 93 § 22
          
        . 

          mad tuat[h]bil dotes, 
            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/&#xC9;riu" title="eDIL Bibliography">Ériu</strong></span> viii 122.14
          
        . arna gentib impadhas 
tuaithbil, na sidhaighi `<span class="quote">the folks that turn withershins, the 
fairies</span>,' 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/ODav." title="eDIL Bibliography">O'Dav.</strong></span> 1600.
         
          do inntódar na troich tuaicbel résan 
teichedh, 
            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Caithr.Thoirdh." title="eDIL Bibliography">Caithr. Thoirdh.</strong></span> 15.18
          
        . an lec sin d'iompod fo 
tri tuaithbhel fair (part of curse left by Maedóc on transgressors), 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/BNn&#xC9;" title="eDIL Bibliography">BNnÉ</strong></span> 248.24
        . 
          impáidis a bachla ┐ a bhuainmionna fo tri túaith-[bél] ina ttimceall, 
            236.34
          
        .</p>
    
  
  
    
      <p class="result"> 
(<span class="edilb">c</span>) With prec. prep. (usually ar, for): 
          impaid in fer 
sin ar tuaithbel ma cuairt, 
            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/YBL" title="eDIL Bibliography">YBL</strong></span> 84a47
          
        . sōais reime ar 
tuáthbel `<span class="quote">turned it lefthandwise</span>' (a mill), 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/BColm." title="eDIL Bibliography">BColm.</strong></span> 58.25
        . 
imasai ar tuaithbel fo thri fair (Satan), 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/&#xC9;riu" title="eDIL Bibliography">Ériu</strong></span> v 122.28
        . 

          imshoi for tuaithbiul ar belaib an tsluaigh, 
            ii 120 § 60
          
        . 

          geis do cuairt ... | for tuath Laighean for tuaith-bel, 
            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Leb.Cert." title="eDIL Bibliography">Leb. Cert.</strong></span> 
12.8
          
        . In more general sense: a ruisc for tuaithbhil `<span class="trans">
          <span class="def">askew</span>
        </span>,' 

          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/MarcoP." title="eDIL Bibliography">Marco P.</strong></span> 187
        . a shuaircfhir sháimh, ní sámh do reacaireacht sceól ... | ar tuathal táid (of ill tidings), 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Hackett" title="eDIL Bibliography">Hackett</strong></span> xii 3
        . With i n-: 
          ticub na thuathbel fó thrí | in topair bi buan can breis, 
            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/&#xC9;riu" title="eDIL Bibliography">Ériu</strong></span> vii 229 § 74
          
         (see under (<span class="edilb">d</span>).</p>
    
  
  
    
      <p class="result"> 
(<span class="edilb">d</span>) As <span class="pos prep." style="color: #ff8000">prep.</span> with gen.: ro gabsat iarom deisiul Temra ┐ 
tuaithbiul Breg <span class="trans">
          <span class="def">lefthandwise</span>
        </span> <span class="ediltr">round B.</span>, 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/LU" title="eDIL Bibliography">LU</strong></span> 6747
         (BDD). 

          lodar tuaithbel Erenn tar Febal ┐ dar Eas Ruaid ... ┐ dar 
Mag n-Ai ┐ dar Mag Cruachan, 
            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/RC" title="eDIL Bibliography">RC</strong></span> xvi 148.19
          
        . 
          cingset 
assin Chrúachain chrúaid | túathbel nÉrenn dar Áth Lúain, 

            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Metr.Dinds." title="eDIL Bibliography">Metr. Dinds.</strong></span> iv 46.2
          
        . 
          imsói tuaithbel in tobuir fo thri, 
            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/RC" title="eDIL Bibliography">RC</strong></span> 
xv 315.13
          
        . 
          tig ... in ainim tuaithbel an cuirp, 
            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/&#xC9;riu" title="eDIL Bibliography">Ériu</strong></span> v 122.44
          
        . 

          tuaithbel na Temhrach, 
            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/BNn&#xC9;" title="eDIL Bibliography">BNnÉ</strong></span> 289.18
          
        .</p>
    
  
  
    
      <p class="result"> 
(<span class="edilb">e</span>) Attrib. <span class="case g">g</span>
        <span class="number s.">s.</span> (usually in fig. meanings): go ndiongnadh

          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/PCT" title="eDIL Bibliography">PCT</strong></span> 644

on an army), 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/CRR" title="eDIL Bibliography">CRR</strong></span> 34.15
        . 
          atad go hanordaighthe imaccuairt, ┐ bleith muilinn tuaithfil orra, 
            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Cog." title="eDIL Bibliography">Cog.</strong></span> 198.z

          do thuit

          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/MR" title="eDIL Bibliography">MR</strong></span> 318.4
