translated `<span class="def">unfaith</span>' in Thes.). In later relig. contexts the 
same, religious <span class="def">doubt</span>, <span class="lbl">etc.</span>:</span> arna tarta amiris . . . dond lobur 
hiressach `<span class="quote">lest he bring unfaith . . . to the weak believer</span>',

            33b5

          31b16

          15a29
