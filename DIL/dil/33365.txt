<div class="row result"><div class="col-sm-3 result_headword"><h3 headword_id="33365">2 ó</h3><span class="citation">Cite this: eDIL s.v. 2 ó or <a class="reference" href="33365">dil.ie/33365</a></span><br/><p style="margin-top: 4px" class="small">Forms:

    <p class="result">
      <span class="pos conj." style="color: #ff8000">conj.</span> Orig. the prep. ó governing a subord. noun-clause

and itself belonging in syntax to the main clause, but later

felt as a conj. belonging to the subord. clause. See Thurn.


        <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Thurn." title="eDIL Bibliography">Thurn. Hdb.</strong></span> § 466
      , 
        <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/PedersenVgl.Gr." title="eDIL Bibliography">Pedersen Vgl. Gr.</strong></span> ii § 511 Anm.
       It must be distinguished from

ó n- (= prep.+ rel.) preceding a subord. clause, <span class="lbl">e.g.</span> hua mbi

<span class="ediltr">from which is</span>,
