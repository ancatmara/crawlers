<div class="row result"><div class="col-sm-3 result_headword"><h3 headword_id="35365">2 ro</h3><span class="citation">Cite this: eDIL s.v. 2 ro or <a class="reference" href="35365">dil.ie/35365</a></span><br/><span class="revision">Last Revised:
                2013</span><br/><p style="margin-top: 4px" class="small">Forms:
                ru, ru, ra, rubrícu, rodoman, roscél, rosét, rosluag, rothorc, ro-Themra</p></div><div class="col-sm-9 result_content">
   
    <p class="result">intens. <span class="pos prefix" style="color: #ff8000">prefix</span> used with adj. and subst., rarely with vb., 
lenites follg. cons. Found in earliest Irish only with adj. (ro, 
ru); in Mid.Ir. common with subst. also (ro, ru, occas. ra).</p>
   
    <p class="result"> 
Largely used in prose and poetry to form unstable compds. 
(oft. merely for the sake of alliteration); the follg. lists are 
merely illustrative.</p>
