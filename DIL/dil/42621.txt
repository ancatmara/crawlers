          <span class="def">lonely</span>, <span class="def">solitary</span>, <span class="def">unfrequented</span>

          adchí uaidhe ... | Eaglais naomhtha i n-áit 
uaignigh,

uaingeach, 
            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/IrishTexts" title="eDIL Bibliography">Irish Texts</strong></span> v 48.25
          
        . 
          docuaidh fein a n-inadh 
uaingech, 
            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/StairErc." title="eDIL Bibliography">Stair Erc.</strong></span> 2448

          go n-éirghidís amach ... ar 
tholchaibh uaigneacha na tíre, 
            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/TSh." title="eDIL Bibliography">TSh.</strong></span> 6033

          isin ditrub uaingech sin, 
            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/DIV2" title="eDIL Bibliography">D IV 2</strong></span>,  72ra1

          ag siubal ... na nditrebh

          a n-díthreabhaibh 
uaighneacha, 
            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/ML" title="eDIL Bibliography">ML</strong></span> 64.16

          aga dícleith 
... i nuarbhothaibh uaiccneacha,

          oirecthar ... tech

        ), 

          fá a gcomair, 
            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/SG" title="eDIL Bibliography">SG</strong></span> 54z

          teagh uaigneach óla i mbaile 
Aodha Í Néill, 
            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/L.Cl.A.B." title="eDIL Bibliography">L. Cl. A. B.</strong></span> 32y
