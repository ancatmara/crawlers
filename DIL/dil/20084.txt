      <span class="itype o-&#x101;">o-ā</span>: the meaning is not certain, but from 
the contexts the most likely suggestion is: <span class="trans">
        <span class="def">decorated</span> with 
piece-work (or patches, either joined together or laid on 
appliqué) so as to form stripes or a chequer or pattern</span>;

5312
