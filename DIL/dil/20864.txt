<div class="row result"><div class="col-sm-3 result_headword"><h3 headword_id="20864">et(a)im, aitim</h3><span class="citation">Cite this: eDIL s.v. et(a)im, aitim or <a class="reference" href="20864">dil.ie/20864</a></span><br/><span class="revision">Last Revised:
                2013</span><br/><p style="margin-top: 4px" class="small">Forms:
                etim, eataim, aitim, etim</p></div><div class="col-sm-9 result_content">
   
    <p class="result">
      <span class="pos n" style="color: #ff8000">n</span> 
      <span class="gen f.">f.</span> (earlier <span class="itype n">n</span>, <span class="gen n.">n.</span> ? *ess-dithaim ?) 
        
          etim, 
eataim, aitim
        , 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/IGT" title="eDIL Bibliography">IGT</strong></span>, Decl. § 13
        
      . The primary meaning is 
uncertain; Meyer's analysis (
        <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Wortk." title="eDIL Bibliography">Wortk.</strong></span> 134
      ) is wrong, as the 
initial is short and the following cons. voiceless (
        etim: etill 

        <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/IGT" title="eDIL Bibliography">IGT</strong></span>, Decl. ex. 636
      . <span class="lbl">Cf.</span> 
        <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/B&#xFC;rgschaft" title="eDIL Bibliography">Bürgschaft</strong></span> p. 12
      ).</p>
   
    
      <p class="result">
        <span class="edilb">I</span> <span class="trans">
          <span class="def">Spring</span>, 
<span class="def">leap</span> (?); <span class="def">thrust</span> (?):</span> 
          rucsat da eitim edtroma fírluatha i certcomdail a chele, 
            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/MR" title="eDIL Bibliography">MR</strong></span> 266.15
          
        . <span class="lbl">Cf.</span> 
          do rug sí eitim is 
sceinnim, 
            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/ITS" title="eDIL Bibliography">ITS</strong></span> iii1 146x
          
        . As <span class="pos vn." style="color: #ff8000">vn.</span>: 
          bui dibergach...oc eitim ┐ 
oc ingreim ar P., 
            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Acall." title="eDIL Bibliography">Acall.</strong></span> 4049
          
        .</p>
    
  
  
    
      <p class="result">
        <span class="edilb">II</span> <span class="trans">
          <span class="def">Chance</span>, <span class="def">opportunity</span>; 
<span class="def">breach</span> (?):</span> 
          eitim .i. báoghal, 
            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/OCl." title="eDIL Bibliography">O'Cl.</strong></span>
          
         ? 
          cach aigni a etaim, 
            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/ZCP" title="eDIL Bibliography">ZCP</strong></span> xvii 50 § 19
          
        , 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Celtica" title="eDIL Bibliography">Celtica</strong></span> xi 24
        . Usually with vb. of 
reaching or seizing: tancatar ┐ rusc mor leo do gabail etma 
for B., ┐ dia doerad iarsein mani hetar im imda acce <span class="ediltr">to take 
B. by surprise</span> (?) 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Thes." title="eDIL Bibliography">Thes.</strong></span> ii 332 z
         = 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Lism.L." title="eDIL Bibliography">Lism. L.</strong></span> p. 320 w
        . 
          ba 
gabáil etma don ríg | immar tharras ina thír, 
            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Metr.Dinds." title="eDIL Bibliography">Metr. Dinds.</strong></span> 
iv 46.49
          
        . 
          ni riacht ceachtar de eitim gona na letartha for 
araile, 
            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Aen." title="eDIL Bibliography">Aen.</strong></span> 3130
          
        . 
          tárraidh mac I. eitim ngabala ar 
dhaghbhaile daingen, 
            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/AFM" title="eDIL Bibliography">AFM</strong></span> v 1674.16
          
        . 
          táraidh E. eitim 
gona ┐ baeguil for P., 
            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/DIV2" title="eDIL Bibliography">D IV 2</strong></span>, 77 [71]va 41
          
        . 
          ar in mbráen 
mar do úair etim | do-chúaidh cráebh d'etill tar ais, 
            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/IGT" title="eDIL Bibliography">IGT</strong></span>, 
Decl. ex. 636
          
        . 
          fuaratar etim ar ghlaslaith an iarla coro 
marbhadh a ngar do ched dibh, 
            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/AFM" title="eDIL Bibliography">AFM</strong></span> v 1600.10
          
        . 
          no gu

            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/IGT" title="eDIL Bibliography">IGT</strong></span>,

        . 
          dus an ccaomhsatais d'eisleis no d'etim rochtain aissidhe go S.,

          d'aitim no

            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Celt.Rev." title="eDIL Bibliography">Celt. Rev.</strong></span> iv 208.21

          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/&#xD3;Bruad." title="eDIL Bibliography">Ó Bruad.</strong></span> iii 150
        .</p>
    
  
  
    
      <p class="result">
        <span class="edilb">III</span> In 
Laws <span class="trans">applied to a species of <span class="def">pledge</span>
        </span>, apparently in a 
transferred sense; the most explicit ex. recorded is: etaim 
.i. gell do thuit ar eit do buith i tir comaithig <span class="ediltr">a pledge 
which is forfeited</span> (?) <span class="ediltr">for cattle being on a neighbour's 
land</span> 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/OMulc." title="eDIL Bibliography">O'Mulc.</strong></span> 467
        . ettim <span class="frn ">ebraice robustoria</span> (?), ar is ar 
niurt berair <span class="lbl">ib.</span> 
          diles cach n-etaim acht diroth cach techta 
iar re ┐ aputh, 
            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/ZCP" title="eDIL Bibliography">ZCP</strong></span> xiii 23 y
          
         (see 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/B&#xFC;rgschaft" title="eDIL Bibliography">Bürgschaft</strong></span> p. 12
        ). 

          leath gacha hathgabala ina hindligedh athgabála...┐ ní tét 
etuim (.i. smacht) tar cúic bú do tuaith, 
            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Laws" title="eDIL Bibliography">Laws</strong></span> i 270.8
          
        . 
          ni 
[<span class="lbl">leg.</span> in ?] mug...ni gaibther an-aetaim fri dlighidh...he,

            104 y

         (&lt; 
          H 3.

         = 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Laws" title="eDIL Bibliography">Laws</strong></span> i 162.11 Comm.

dilsi don flaith .i. narup gat na aurrach `<span class="quote">mit ihren Verschul-
dungen</span>' 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/ZCP" title="eDIL Bibliography">ZCP</strong></span> xvi 206
         (= 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/OCurry" title="eDIL Bibliography">O'Curry</strong></span> 828
         &lt; 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/H3.18" title="eDIL Bibliography">H 3.18</strong></span>, 380a
          
        ). In

which a certain fine was due.</p>
