adjj. compounded with il- and those derived from compound 
nouns of which il- is the first element, <span class="lbl">e.g.</span> ilchenélach,

between them. There seems to be a distinction in meaning. 
il-, in the true compounds, having for the most part 
merely intensive force, <span class="lbl">e.g.</span> il-bind, il-chrúaid, il-gránda.

nouns no hyphen is used here.
