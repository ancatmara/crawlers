    <p class="result"> eighth letter of the Irish alphabet and first letter of the 
second or <i>h</i> group in the Ogam alphabet, 
        <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Auraic." title="eDIL Bibliography">Auraic.</strong></span> 1138, 4229
      , 

        <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/IGT" title="eDIL Bibliography">IGT</strong></span> Introd. § 4

        § 17
       (
        <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Auraic." title="eDIL Bibliography">Auraic.</strong></span> 3559

        <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/LewisShort" title="eDIL Bibliography">Lewis &amp; Short</strong></span> <span class="lbl">s.v.</span>
      ). <span class="lbl">Cf.</span> 
        <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Graim." title="eDIL Bibliography">Graim.</strong></span> 2899 02

        <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Speculum" title="eDIL Bibliography">Speculum</strong></span> xxiv 28

for Latin <span class="frn ">autem</span>). Not in earlier ogam inscriptions in Irish, 
Macalister, <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/CIIC" title="eDIL Bibliography">Corpus Inscriptionum Insularum Celticarum</strong></span> i

       and note. 
Indicates length in prec. vowel in Ternohc (= Ternóc), 

        289.18
      .</p>
   
    <p class="result">In O. Ir. initial (and rarely medial) <i>h</i> is without phonetic 
value, 
        <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/GOI" title="eDIL Bibliography">GOI</strong></span> 19
