which cannot be <span class="def">answered</span>, has <span class="def">nothing</span> <span class="def">corresponding</span> to it</span> (see

<span class="def">unparalleled</span>, <span class="def">irresistible</span> (?), <span class="def">immense</span>, associated with adjs. 
denoting size or quantity;</span> used of persons, things, actions,
