<div class="row result"><div class="col-sm-3 result_headword"><h3 headword_id="32754">muinter</h3><span class="citation">Cite this: eDIL s.v. muinter or <a class="reference" href="32754">dil.ie/32754</a></span><br/><p style="margin-top: 4px" class="small">Forms:
                muntar, montar, montar, muinter, monater-, moniter-, montar, manu-tera, muntar, montar, muinter, muntair, muntir, muintir, muntaire, muntairai, muntire, muintire, montar, muinter, muinntear, muinter, muintera</p></div><div class="col-sm-9 result_content">
   
    <p class="result">
      <span class="pos n" style="color: #ff8000">n</span> 
      <span class="itype &#x101;">ā</span>, <span class="gen f.">f.</span>, O.Ir. also muntar, montar. A word of disputed 
derivation; (<i>a</i>) acc. to Thurneysen, 
        <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Thurn." title="eDIL Bibliography">Thurn. Hdb.</strong></span> § 905
      , montar and 
muinter represent respectively monater- and moniter- &lt; Lat. 
<span class="frn ">monasterium (monis-</span>); so also Hessen, 
        <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/ZCP" title="eDIL Bibliography">ZCP</strong></span> ix 11
      , Pokorny, 

        <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/ZCP" title="eDIL Bibliography">ZCP</strong></span> x 203.
       (<i>b</i>) acc. to de Jubainville, 
        <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/RC" title="eDIL Bibliography">RC</strong></span> xxv 2
      , montar 
(muntar) &lt; *manu-tera `<span class="quote">that which is under the hand, <span class="lbl">i.e.</span> the 
authority</span>'. (<i>c</i>) Vendryes, 
        <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/ZCP" title="eDIL Bibliography">ZCP</strong></span> ix 296
       suggests a native origin, 
Celt. *<span class="frn ">moni</span> cogn. with Ir. muin `<span class="ediltr">protection</span>', see <span class="ref"><a class="normal" href="search?search_in=headword&amp;q=1 muin I (c)">1 muin (c)</a></span> 
above.</p>

                if (!$(this).is(e.target) && $(this).has(e.target).length === 0 && $('.popover').has(e.target).length === 0) {
                    $(this).popover('destroy');
                }
            });
        });
	  })
</script>
