                2019</span><br/><p style="margin-top: 4px" class="small">Forms:
                taidbrid, taidhbhred, taidbred, tedbarthae</p></div><div class="col-sm-9 result_content">
   

    <p class="result">Analysis doubtful. The forms in Wb. and Ml. 
indicate *to-ad-uss-ber- (see Strachan, Paradigms and 
Glosses), but the later forms and vn. may orig. belong to 
*to-ad-fíri (see <span class="ref"><a class="normal" href="search?search_in=headword&amp;q=ad-f&#xED;ri">ad-fíri</a></span>). Later as simple <span class="pos vb." style="color: #ff8000">vb.</span> taidbrid.
