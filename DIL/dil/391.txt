    <p class="result">
      <span class="pos v" style="color: #ff8000">v</span> 
      <span class="trans">
        <span class="def">sees</span>.</span> Frequ. (outside O. Ir. Glosses) at-c(h)í with 
meaningless infix. pron., in <span style="color: #ff8000">rel.</span> with inf. pron. sometimes 
also as-. Prot. stem -a(i)cc-. In O. Ir. with depon. inflexion in subj. pres. and with redupl. future. For active inflexion in subj. see 
        <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Celt.Verb" title="eDIL Bibliography">Celt. Verb</strong></span> 138
      . In pret. active 
prot. forms only are found, the conjunction co n- being used 
where no other conjunct particle is present (deuterotonic 
forms occur in pass.). In perf. deut. with root -derc- and 
perfective -com- (ad-condarc <span class="lbl">etc.</span>). There is no special perf. 
form after conj. particles. Note also ad-rodarcar <span class="trans">can be <span class="def">seen</span>
      </span>, 

        <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Sg." title="eDIL Bibliography">Sg.</strong></span> 172a2
      . In later lang. ad- (at-) is gradually replaced by do- 
and the conj. stem acquires inorganic f-, giving the forms 
do-chí, -faic-, both with <span class="tns b-fut.">b-fut.</span>. In <span class="tns past">past</span> tense the general 
independent form is ad-(do-) chonnai(r)c (from the <span class="tns perf.">perf.</span>), the 
form after conj. particles -(f)ac(c)- (from the <span class="tns pret.">pret.</span>), both 
with endings of the <span class="tns s-pret.">s-pret.</span>.</p>
