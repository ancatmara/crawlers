<div class="row result"><div class="col-sm-3 result_headword"><h3 headword_id="42198">túalaing</h3><span class="citation">Cite this: eDIL s.v. túalaing or <a class="reference" href="42198">dil.ie/42198</a></span><br/><span class="revision">Last Revised:
                2019</span><br/><p style="margin-top: 4px" class="small">Forms:

      <span class="pos adj" style="color: #ff8000">adj</span> Perh. <span class="itype i">i</span>- stem, but O. Ir. <span class="case n">n</span>
      <span class="number s.">s.</span> túalang. <span class="itype s">s</span>- stem, 

        <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/PedersenVgl.Gr." title="eDIL Bibliography">Pedersen Vgl. Gr.</strong></span> ii 117
      , but 
        tuailnge,
