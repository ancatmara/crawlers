ORIGIN: From †di-ecmaing, <span class="lbl">i.e.</span> privative di- + ecmang, 
 <span class="pos vn." style="color: #ff8000">vn.</span> of <span class="ref"><a class="normal" href="search?search_in=headword&amp;q=1 ad-cumaing">1 ad-cumaing</a></span> `<span class="ediltr">it happens, takes place</span>,' the <span class="itype o">o</span>-stem 
passing into an <span class="itype i">i</span>-stem in composition, <span class="lbl">cf.</span> <span class="ref"><a class="normal" href="search?search_in=headword&amp;q=deccair">deccair</a></span>, <span class="ref"><a class="normal" href="search?search_in=headword&amp;q=1 deithbir">1 deithbir</a></span>, 
<span class="ref"><a class="normal" href="search?search_in=headword&amp;q=deolaid">deolaid</a></span>, <span class="ref"><a class="normal" href="search?search_in=headword&amp;q=d&#xED;&#xE1;irim">díáirim</a></span>, <span class="ref"><a class="normal" href="search?search_in=headword&amp;q=D&#xED;armait">Díarmait</a></span>, and especially the synonymous 
<span class="ref"><a class="normal" href="search?search_in=headword&amp;q=d&#xED;ing">díing</a></span>.</p>
