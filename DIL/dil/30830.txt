<div class="row result"><div class="col-sm-3 result_headword"><h3 headword_id="30830">lúamnach, lúaimnech</h3><span class="citation">Cite this: eDIL s.v. lúamnach, lúaimnech or <a class="reference" href="30830">dil.ie/30830</a></span><br/><p style="margin-top: 4px" class="small">Forms:
                luaimneach</p></div><div class="col-sm-9 result_content">
   
    <p class="result">
      <span class="pos adj" style="color: #ff8000">adj</span> 
      <span class="itype o,-&#x101;">o, ā</span> (lúamain). In later lang. with 
unlen. -m-, see 
        <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Duan.F." title="eDIL Bibliography">Duan. F.</strong></span> Gloss
      . (but luaimneach: 
        roúaibhreach, 
          ii 84 § 63
        
      ). 
        luaimneach, 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Eg.Gl." title="eDIL Bibliography">Eg. Gl.</strong></span> 428.
        
      
    </p>
   
    
      <p class="result"> 
(<span class="edilb">a</span>) <span class="trans">
          <span class="def">volatile</span>, <span class="def">flying</span>, <span class="def">fluttering</span>, <span class="def">moving</span>, <span class="def">active</span>. etc.</span> Of birds 
and animals: 
          sénais inn ēn lūamnech (luamnach <span class="lbl">v.l.</span>), 
            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Hy." title="eDIL Bibliography">Hy.</strong></span> 
v 64
          
        . ech . . . lúamnach <span class="ediltr">prancing</span>, 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/LU" title="eDIL Bibliography">LU</strong></span> 8659
         (
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/FB" title="eDIL Bibliography">FB</strong></span> 49
        ). aire 
ad luamnaigh (of bees), 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Laws" title="eDIL Bibliography">Laws</strong></span> iv 114.24 Comm
        . nathraig 
lúamnig `<span class="quote">flying serpents</span>', 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/&#xC9;riu" title="eDIL Bibliography">Ériu</strong></span> ii 196.4
        . 
          is é an aicil luaimnech, 

            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/TBCSt." title="eDIL Bibliography">TBC St.</strong></span> 4423.
          
         Of persons: 
          fri L. lúamnach, 
            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Metr.Dinds." title="eDIL Bibliography">Metr. Dinds.</strong></span> 
iii 200.24
          
        . 
          Molling luaimnech, 
            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/SG" title="eDIL Bibliography">SG</strong></span> 385.41
          
         (see 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/&#xC9;igse" title="eDIL Bibliography">Éigse</strong></span> vi 97
        ). 

          ar in ri luamnach din leirg,

        . L. lúaimhneach 
`<span class="quote">the Swift</span>', 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Duan.F." title="eDIL Bibliography">Duan. F.</strong></span> ii 142 § 6
