<div class="row result"><div class="col-sm-3 result_headword"><h3 headword_id="27638">imma n-</h3><span class="citation">Cite this: eDIL s.v. imma n- or <a class="reference" href="27638">dil.ie/27638</a></span><br/><span class="revision">Last Revised:
                2019</span><br/><p style="margin-top: 4px" class="small">Forms:
                immanarraid</p></div><div class="col-sm-9 result_content">
   


    <p class="result">reflexive and reciprocal <span class="pos preverb" style="color: #ff8000">preverb</span> used before compound vbs. to form new compounds. The an
       may be a 
petrified poss. pron. p. 3, or an infix. pron. s. 3 m. It is 
used impersonally, (i) with the prep. DO <span class="lbl">e.g.</span> 
        ceni immonacai 
dun, 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/IT" title="eDIL Bibliography">IT</strong></span> ii2 190.2
        
      . (ii) with the first noun (or pronoun) 
introduced by the prep. DO, the second with FRI <span class="lbl">e.g.</span>
      
         immanarnic dosom frisin Liath Macha, 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/LU" title="eDIL Bibliography">LU</strong></span> 8458
        
      , or in ordinary personal use <span class="lbl">e.g.</span> 
        immanaiccet int shlúaig, 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/TBFr." title="eDIL Bibliography">TBFr.</strong></span> 176.
        
       
See 
        <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/PedersenVgl.Gr." title="eDIL Bibliography">Pedersen Vgl. Gr.</strong></span> § 552 (4)
      , 
        §589 (3)
      ; 
        <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/GOI" title="eDIL Bibliography">GOI</strong></span> § 841 D
      ; 
O'Brien, 
        <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/&#xC9;riu" title="eDIL Bibliography">Ériu</strong></span> xii 242 ff
      .</p>
