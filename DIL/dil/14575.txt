      <p class="result"> 
 <span class="edilb">I</span> <span class="trans">Of living beings or their attributives: <span class="def">stout-hearted</span>, 
<span class="def">courageous</span>, <span class="def">daring</span>, <span class="def">fearless</span>; emphasized: <span class="def">bold</span>, <span class="def">brave</span>
        </span>; the 
opposite of etánae, uaimnech, immecal, eclach `timid, 
fearful,' and often synonymous with essamain, trén, calmae, 
slattrae. It translates Lat. <span class="frn ">audax, temer</span>, and <span class="frn ">confidens</span>. 
Laws and Glosses offer no example.</p>
