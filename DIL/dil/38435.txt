      <span class="itype i&#x101;">iā</span>, <span class="gen f.">f.</span> (somaín) <span class="trans">collective term for <span class="def">dues</span> or <span class="def">services</span> <span class="def">rendered</span> by a <span class="def">client</span>
      </span> (if they are properly or justly imposed, <span class="lbl">i.e.</span> 
correspond to the value of his holding), see Thurn. 
        <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/ZCP" title="eDIL Bibliography">ZCP</strong></span> xiv

flaithemnasa a somoínib sét `<span class="quote">which bring into play the divisions 
of nobility according to the varying revenues from their property</span>
