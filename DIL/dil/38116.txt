      <span class="pos v" style="color: #ff8000">v</span> First vowel prob. short in O.Ir. (see 
        <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/GOI" title="eDIL Bibliography">GOI</strong></span> 
§ 47
      ). In later lang. freq. snáidid, snáigid, and also often with 
stem snaí- (diphthong attested by rhyme in bardic verse, see 

        <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/IGT" title="eDIL Bibliography">IGT</strong></span> Verbs § 42
      ). On pret. see 
        <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/GOI" title="eDIL Bibliography">GOI</strong></span> § 680
      . Redupl. s-pret. 
        ro ṡenas (MSS. re henaus, re&lt; &gt;naus), 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/&#xC9;riu" title="eDIL Bibliography">Ériu</strong></span> 52, 60.11, 63
        
      .</p>
