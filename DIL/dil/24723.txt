<div class="row result"><div class="col-sm-3 result_headword"><h3 headword_id="24723">fúathróc</h3><span class="citation">Cite this: eDIL s.v. fúathróc or <a class="reference" href="24723">dil.ie/24723</a></span><br/><p style="margin-top: 4px" class="small">Forms:
                fuathbróc, fuathroic, fuathroga</p></div><div class="col-sm-9 result_content">
   
    <p class="result">
      <span class="pos n" style="color: #ff8000">n</span>  <span class="itype &#x101;">ā</span>, <span class="gen f.">f.</span> also fuathbróc (<span class="lbl">cf.</span> <span class="ref"><a class="normal" href="search?search_in=headword&amp;q=br&#xF3;c">bróc</a></span>), the usual form in 
LL (
        fúathróc, 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/LL" title="eDIL Bibliography">LL</strong></span>
           237b28
        
      ).</p>
   
    
      <p class="result"> 
(<span class="edilb">a</span>) <span class="trans">a <span class="def">girdle</span> or <span class="def">apron</span>
        </span> worn round the waist and loins by 
warriors, of leather or silk or other material, often richly 
ornamented: 
          cen erriud ... acht fuathbróc dondlethair 
degṡúata cu airbaccaib a dā ochsal, 
            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/LL" title="eDIL Bibliography">LL</strong></span> 266b45
          
         (
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/MU2" title="eDIL Bibliography">MU²</strong></span> 644
        ). 

          ni raibhi d'erradh uime acht fuathrog ... srollaidhi ima 
ichtur ┐ leine ... imma ghelchnes, 
            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/StairErc." title="eDIL Bibliography">Stair Erc.</strong></span> 171
          
        . 
          fuathrog 
ḟairsing urleathair um deanacht a c[h]uirp, 
            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/CF" title="eDIL Bibliography">CF</strong></span> 199
          
        . 
          rogabastár a úathroic srebnaide sroill cona cimais do banór ... 
fri moethichtur a medóin, 
            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/LU" title="eDIL Bibliography">LU</strong></span> 6420
          
         (= 
          úathbróic, 
            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/TBC-LL1" title="eDIL Bibliography">TBC-LL¹</strong></span> 2565
          
        ). 

          cona chathchris ... dara fúathróic ... sechtair, 
            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/LU" title="eDIL Bibliography">LU</strong></span>
             6424
          
        . 
          cliabinar ré chnes co ngebethar do co barrúachtar a dondfúathroci donddergi mileta do srol ríg, 
            6558
          
         (= dondfúathbróci 

          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/TBC-LL1" title="eDIL Bibliography">TBC-LL¹</strong></span> 2735
        ). 
          bernḟuathbróc srethach srollaide ... congeib 
ar tiug a bánṡliasat co cumtuch a chuirp, 
            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/LL" title="eDIL Bibliography">LL</strong></span> 189a51
          
        . 
          fúathrog bhuilidh bhreacṡróil, 
            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/BS" title="eDIL Bibliography">BS</strong></span> 104y
          
        . 
          do gab a errad 
comraic uime ... .i. inar ... ┐ an mbanḟuatroic mbrecṡolus 
do or ... re imtus a medoin, 
            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/&#xC9;riu" title="eDIL Bibliography">Ériu</strong></span> iii 170 § 28
          
        . Of metal: 

          ra gabastar a ḟuathbróic n-imdangin ... n-iarnaide do iurn 
... ar uamun in gae bulga, 
            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/TBC-LL1" title="eDIL Bibliography">TBC-LL¹</strong></span> 3729
          
        . </p>
     
      <p class="result">

          gabais
