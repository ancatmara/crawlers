      <p class="result">(<span class="edilb">a</span>) trans. and intrans. <span class="trans">

        </span>: 
          toingid . . . na ro siubail in

            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/OCurry" title="eDIL Bibliography">O'Curry</strong></span> 622
          
         (
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/H3.18" title="eDIL Bibliography">H 3.18</strong></span>, 
p. 301
        ). 
          airrter na tíre tarrneas; | siblaidh fo crech is fo cain, 

            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/MacCarthy" title="eDIL Bibliography">MacCarthy</strong></span> 126.25

          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/RSl&#xE1;inte" title="eDIL Bibliography">RSláinte</strong></span> 549
        .

doman soir no siar, ┐ ni síubhuil in conuir so . . . æn ridiri . . ., 

            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/RC" title="eDIL Bibliography">RC</strong></span> x 190.5

        .

          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Maund." title="eDIL Bibliography">Maund.</strong></span> 2
