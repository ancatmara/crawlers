      <span class="pos v" style="color: #ff8000">v</span> (béo) also béogaid <span class="trans">makes alive, <span class="def">vivifies</span>, <span class="def">animates</span>:</span> 

        beoigidir in spirut in corp, 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Wb." title="eDIL Bibliography">Wb.</strong></span> 13d7
        
      . is iress Crist nombeoigedar `<span class="quote">quickens me</span>', 
        19a20
      . turgbhat na huisci tondaitecu 
na hanma beoaigheas (= <span class="frn ">animae viventis</span>, 
        <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Bedel" title="eDIL Bibliography">Gen.</strong></span> i 20
      ), 
        <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/BB" title="eDIL Bibliography">BB</strong></span> 
15b23
       see <span class="ref"><a class="normal" href="search?search_in=headword&amp;q=1 tonn">1 tonn I (c)</a></span> . béoghuidh mé ann do shlighe = <span class="ediltr">quicken 
me in Thy ways</span>, 
        <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Bedel" title="eDIL Bibliography">Psalms</strong></span> cxix 37.
       <span class="trans">brings back to life, <span class="def">revives</span>; <span class="def">renews</span>:</span> 
beoaigter fri oethu airm irro trebsat mairm (<span class="lbl">leg.</span> mairb) `<span class="quote">let 
the dead be made to live</span>', 
        <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/LU" title="eDIL Bibliography">LU</strong></span> 3478

        <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/SCC2" title="eDIL Bibliography">SCC²</strong></span> 275 n
      .). <span class="lbl">Cf.</span> 
        beoaighther oitiu .i. beoaighter do é i naititid,
