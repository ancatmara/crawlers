      <p class="result">(<span class="edilb">a</span>) <span class="trans">
          <span class="def">day</span>, <span class="def">daylight</span>
        </span> (as distinguished from night: see 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Celtica" title="eDIL Bibliography">Celtica</strong></span> iii 256-7
        ): 
          samaltir in molad dóinde fri laithe ar a gairti ṁbis,

        . 
          intan tét a laithe di chiunn, 
            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Ml." title="eDIL Bibliography">Ml.</strong></span> 21c3
          
        . 
          amal as toisegiu grián indáas laithe ┐ is laithe foilsigedar cech rét, 
            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Ml." title="eDIL Bibliography">Ml.</strong></span> 85b11
          
        . 
          adaig tar aesi laíthi,

            58d3

            301
          
        . 
          is ī rē laithe nobitis isin cess,

          ba hiat sin na sē laithi |. . . lūan, mairt, cēdāin, is dardāin | āine [┐] satharn, 
            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/&#xC9;riu" title="eDIL Bibliography">Ériu</strong></span> iii 143.6

      <p class="result">With adaig:
