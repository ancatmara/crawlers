    <p class="result"> 
In later times certain spots were popularly called fulachta 
Fian (na bhF.) : 
        do bhíodh do mhéid na dteinnte-se go 
bhfuilid a láithreacha dubhloiscthe i mórán d'áitibh i 
nÉirinn aniú ┐ is diobh ghairmid na criadhaireadha Fulacht 
Fian, 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Keat." title="eDIL Bibliography">Keat.</strong></span> ii 5113
        
      . </p>
  
  
    <p class="result">
