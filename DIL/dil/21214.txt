      <span class="pos n" style="color: #ff8000">n</span>  <span class="itype i&#x101;">iā</span>, <span class="gen f.">f.</span> Does not occur in Glosses. The 
form with -d- is common in LL, beside faith- and faig-. 
It may well be the earlier form. <span class="trans">A <span class="def">green</span> or <span class="def">lawn</span> in front of a 
fort or <span class="def">residence</span> or church; an <span class="def">open</span> <span class="def">space</span> in or before a city; public <span class="def">square</span>, <span class="def">courtyard</span>
      </span> (
        <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Celtica" title="eDIL Bibliography">Celtica</strong></span> xviii 89
      ); <span class="trans">a green <span class="def">meadow</span>, or <span class="def">playingfield</span>;</span> sometimes referred to as a place for cattle: fait[h]che .i. feth-chaí .i. conair iarna
