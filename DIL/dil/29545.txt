Legal: cáin lánamnae <span class="ediltr">law of relationships between couples</span>, 
see 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Stud.inE.Ir.Law" title="eDIL Bibliography">Stud. in E. Ir. Law</strong></span> i § 1

also referred to: 
          cis lir lanamna do cuisin la Feine? . . . a ocht: 
flaith fri aicillne, eclais fria manchu, athair fria ingin, . . . fer

            § 2. 
          
        Of numbers of persons attending men of 
different ranks on circuit: 
          deich lánamna[i] a chórus for coí
