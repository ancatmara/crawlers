    <p class="result">
      <span class="pos v" style="color: #ff8000">v</span> (<span class="ref"><a class="normal" href="search?search_in=headword&amp;q=1 l&#xFA;ath">1 lúath</a></span>). 
        luathachadh, 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/IGT" title="eDIL Bibliography">IGT</strong></span> Verbs § 106 (245.6)
        
      .</p>
   
    
      <p class="result"> 
(<span class="edilb">a</span>) Intrans. <span class="trans">
          <span class="def">hurries</span>, <span class="def">makes</span> <span class="def">haste</span>, <span class="def">goes</span> <span class="def">faster</span>
        </span>: gor' luathaigh a 
anal <span class="ediltr">his breath grew swift</span>, 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/CCath." title="eDIL Bibliography">CCath.</strong></span> 2955.
         ? 
          gilla nār lūaithigh 
re lā, 
            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/ZCP" title="eDIL Bibliography">ZCP</strong></span> viii 227 § 10.
          
         Usually with pred. introduced 
by prep.: lúathaigh ort, ainic misi <span class="ediltr">make haste</span>, 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/&#xC9;riu" title="eDIL Bibliography">Ériu</strong></span> ix 2 § 5.
         

          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/DD&#xE1;na" title="eDIL Bibliography">DDána</strong></span> 88.36
        . lúathaigheadh ar a obair `<span class="quote">let him . . . hasten 
his work</span>', 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Bedel" title="eDIL Bibliography">Isaiah</strong></span> v 19
        . luathaighthear leat ar an luibh `<span class="quote">make

          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/EachtraUill." title="eDIL Bibliography">Eachtra Uill.</strong></span> 462
