<span class="ref"><a class="normal" href="search?search_in=headword&amp;q=sra&#xED;nid">sraínid</a></span>) <span class="trans">

      </span>, both intrans. 
and trans., with FOR of vanquished and (in intrans. use) RÉ of 
victor. raena .i. sraoineadh no briseadh; ro raena reamhainn 
.i. go mbrise, O'Cl. (see
