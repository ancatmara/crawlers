<span class="trans">a <span class="def">fief of stock or land</span> given by a chief or landlord to a tribesman 
or tenant subject to certain counter-obligations (divided 
mainly into doír-rath and soír-rath
      </span>); by Thurneysen, 
        <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/C&#xF3;icCon." title="eDIL Bibliography">Cóic Con.</strong></span> p. 9

fognam do denam uada, 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/C&#xF3;icCon." title="eDIL Bibliography">Cóic Con.</strong></span> 54 § 123
        
       (the `fuidir'

required of him, he must perform it or restore the loan). 

        dilis, acht cu muine coire, rath n-athigh do flaith,
