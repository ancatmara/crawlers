    <p class="result">
      <span class="pos v" style="color: #ff8000">v</span> (Mid.Ir. finnaid) <span class="trans">
        <span class="def">finds</span> out, <span class="def">discovers</span>
      </span>. In O.Ir. 
a depon.; three different stems are used in conjugation: 
(1) finn- `<span class="quote">discover</span>', in imper., indic. pres. and imperf. (2) 
fes(s)-, fias- in subj., fut. and cond., also pass. pret. and gerundive. (3) fit-, fet- in pret. act. with force of pres. `<span class="quote">know</span>'. For 
-fitir see 
        <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/ZCP" title="eDIL Bibliography">ZCP</strong></span> xv 204
      ; 
        <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/NorsktidsskriftforSprogvidenskap" title="eDIL Bibliography">Norsk tidsskrift for Sprogvidenskap</strong></span> iv 426
      ; 
        <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/IndogermanischeForschungen" title="eDIL Bibliography">Indogermanische Forschungen</strong></span> xxxix 220
      . For the form fo-retar see 
        <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/&#xC9;riu" title="eDIL Bibliography">Ériu</strong></span> xii 222
      ; 
for forms without ro- in answering a question see <span class="lbl">ibid.</span> 
        219
      .</p>
