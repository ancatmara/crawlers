      <p class="result"> 
<span class="edilb">I</span>
      </p>
     
      
        <p class="result">(<span class="edilb">a</span>) <span class="trans">in lit. sense <span class="def">is around</span>, <span class="def">is about</span>:</span> donaib cenelaib 
immerabtar iudeu `<span class="quote">to the nations who were about the Jews</span>,' 

            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Ml." title="eDIL Bibliography">Ml.</strong></span> 37a16
          . a himtomas i nde i seilb imebiad <span class="ediltr">it should be 
measured in two directions to the property of those around</span>, 

            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Laws" title="eDIL Bibliography">Laws</strong></span> iv 142.14 Comm.
           
            na ceithri comithaigh íma biad ocus 
na ceitri ecomithaigh ada neasam doibside, 
              128.1 Comm.
            
           
fris na cethri comaithchiu ata nesom immidbiat fri da 
taeb <span class="ediltr">who are nearest about it</span>, 
            i 260.11
          . crich do teid uisce 
glaise airm fo leanad in da comarba imdabiad for cechtar 
in da leithe `<span class="quote">where the two `coarbs' follow it, they being on 
either side of it</span>,' 
            iv 144.11 Comm.
           
            co nderna rí Locha Léin 
cáin for cach tūeth immidmbē etir Corco Bascind ┐ Corcomruad, 
              <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/ZCP" title="eDIL Bibliography">ZCP</strong></span> viii 316.21
            
          .</p>
      
     
      
        <p class="result"> 
(<span class="edilb">b</span>) In metaph. sense : 
            foídid Cu Chulaind atheroch 
Láeg do fis scél dús cia cruth imtháthar (mbotha, 
              <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/TBC-I1" title="eDIL Bibliography">TBC-I¹</strong></span> 
1572
            
          ) isin dunud <span class="ediltr">to see how things are going in the camp</span>, 

            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/LU" title="eDIL Bibliography">LU</strong></span> 6043.
          
        </p>
      
    
  
  
    
      <p class="result"> 
<span class="edilb">II</span> From lit. meaning <span class="ediltr">is about, is alongside of</span> is developed 
<span class="trans">
          figur. meaning <span class="def">is so</span>, <span class="def">even so</span>, <span class="def">thus</span>
        </span> (it is often followed redundantly by samlaid which becomes general as the meaning of the verb is gradually forgotten) : imta lanamnas 
tothla i taide `<span class="quote">the connexion of secret elopement is similar</span>,' 

          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Laws" title="eDIL Bibliography">Laws</strong></span> ii 402.3
         glossed 
          .i. is amlaidh sin ata in lanamnus, 

            <span class="lbl">ib.</span> 19 Comm.
          
         imtha dano techta fuillema gill cacha fir 
rinnas `<span class="quote">so also is the lawful pledge-interest of each man who 
satirises</span>,' 
          v 388.4
        . 
          imta fri cín comfocais, 
            iv 38.16
          
        . ? <span class="number pl.">pl.</span> 

          imtat na fogla im comaidchius `<span class="quote">in the same way trespasses 
in cotenancy</span>' (Plummer MS. notes), 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/OCurry" title="eDIL Bibliography">O'Curry</strong></span> 859
         (&lt; 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/H3.18" title="eDIL Bibliography">H 3.18</strong></span>, 
p. 388b
          
        ). ? To this : <span class="tns pres.">pres.</span> <span class="mood subj.">subj.</span> 
          immusbé fadēin fon oen

          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/RC" title="eDIL Bibliography">RC</strong></span> xxvi 52 § 282.
