<span class="edilb">I</span> <span class="trans">
          <span class="def">Between</span>
        </span> (two individuals or groups)</p>
     
      
        <p class="result">
          <span class="edilb">(a)</span> of spatial, 
also of personal, moral and reciprocal relations. For convenience of reference the exx. in this subsection are classified as follows: (a) the objects of the prep. are denoted 
respectively by the suff. or independent pron. and a subst.; 
(b) the objj. are denoted by suff. pron.; (c) each of the objj. 
is a subst. (sg. or pl.) or each is a pron.; (d) the objj. are 
denoted by a dual subst.; (e) a pl. or collect. subst.</p>
