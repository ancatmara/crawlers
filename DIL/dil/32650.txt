<div class="row result"><div class="col-sm-3 result_headword"><h3 headword_id="32650">múchaid</h3><span class="citation">Cite this: eDIL s.v. múchaid or <a class="reference" href="32650">dil.ie/32650</a></span><br/><span class="revision">Last Revised:
                2019</span><br/><p style="margin-top: 4px" class="small">Forms:
                múgsat, múchad</p></div><div class="col-sm-9 result_content">
   
    <p class="result">
      <span class="pos v" style="color: #ff8000">v</span> 
      <span class="itype &#x101;">ā</span>, (múch).</p>
   
    <p class="result"> 
The primary meaning may be that of stifling by smoke, 
<span class="lbl">etc.</span>; hence variously <span class="trans">
        <span class="def">covers over</span>, <span class="def">presses down</span>; <span class="def">suffocates</span>; 
<span class="def">quenches</span>, <span class="def">extinguishes</span> (fire, <span class="lbl">etc.</span>);</span> <span class="trans">
        <span class="def">oppresses</span>; <span class="def">suppresses</span>, <span class="def">obliterates</span>.</span> <span class="tns pret.">pret.</span> 
        ru múgsat nó ro cechladatar, 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Wb." title="eDIL Bibliography">Wb.</strong></span> 5a24
        
      ; gl. 
altaria tua suffuderunt (= <span class="frn ">suffōderunt</span>); the first gloss is prob. 
meant for <span class="frn ">suffundere</span>, the second for <span class="frn ">suffodere</span>. 
        muchais C. 
a étan fri lár, 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/LU" title="eDIL Bibliography">LU</strong></span> 1536
