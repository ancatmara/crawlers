<div class="row result"><div class="col-sm-3 result_headword"><h3 headword_id="3913">1 ara</h3><span class="citation">Cite this: eDIL s.v. 1 ara or <a class="reference" href="3913">dil.ie/3913</a></span><br/><p style="margin-top: 4px" class="small">Forms:
                ar</p></div><div class="col-sm-9 result_content">
  
  
    <p class="result">
      <span class="pos conj." style="color: #ff8000">conj.</span>, nasalising, folld. by subj. See 
        <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/GOI" title="eDIL Bibliography">GOI</strong></span> 
§ 898
      . Reduced to ar before the neg. ná, before forms of 
the copula (often as air, see 
        <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/GOI" title="eDIL Bibliography">GOI</strong></span> § 803
      ), before 
inf. pron. (
        <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Wb." title="eDIL Bibliography">Wb.</strong></span> 12a3
      ) and in the later ar co; note also a
        rnacathar, 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Ml." title="eDIL Bibliography">Ml.</strong></span> 68b9
        
      .</p>
  
  
    
      <p class="result"> 
<span class="edilb">(a)</span> As final <span class="pos conj." style="color: #ff8000">conj.</span> <span class="trans">
          <span class="def">that</span>, <span class="def">in order that</span>
        </span>. Translating Lat. 
<span class="frn ">ut, ne</span> with subjunctive: arimp do mórad dǽ uile gl. ut 
gratia . . . abundet in gloriam Dei, 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Wb." title="eDIL Bibliography">Wb.</strong></span> 15c4
        . arinchomalnathar cach ṅgrád gl. ut . . . ornent in omnibus, 
          31c14
        . 
arimpfollus gl. ut . . . euidens fiat, 
          32a2
        . arna derlind gl. 
ne fratrem meum scandalizem, 
          10c14
        . arna foircnea for 
crabud and gl. ne ab inuicem consummemini, 
          20b13
        . arna 
ecmailtigetar gl. né insolescant, 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Ml." title="eDIL Bibliography">Ml.</strong></span> 27c6
        .</p>
     
      <p class="result"> 
Continuing the sense of a Latin ipv.: arnachitrindarpither 
frisin fírini <span class="frn ">per superbiam</span>
         gl. noli altum sapere sed time, 

          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Wb." title="eDIL Bibliography">Wb.</strong></span> 5b33
        . armbat irlithiu dé <span class="frn ">domini mandatís dei</span>
         gl. serui 
obaedite dominís carnalibus, 
          22d2
        . arnap éicen a ngóire in 
æclis gl. . . . non grauetur aeclesia, 
          29a10
        .</p>
     
      <p class="result"> 
<span class="trans">
          <span class="def">In order that</span>, <span class="def">so that</span> in general:</span> gigestesi dia linn arafulsam 
ar fochidi <span class="ediltr">in order that we may bear</span>, 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Wb." title="eDIL Bibliography">Wb.</strong></span> 14c2a
        . 
          is dobar

            16a24
          
        . 
          arnaroib na 
eícmailt i n-ellug inna æcaillse, 
            22c20
          
        . arna farcabtis cen 
forcitlaid <span class="ediltr">that they might have a teacher</span>, 
          31d13
        . arna roib 
cummasc foir <span class="ediltr">to avoid confusion</span>, 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Sg." title="eDIL Bibliography">Sg.</strong></span> 2a8
        . 
          mo chorp rop 
sigith sóethrach / arnādrīs iffernn, 
            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Thes." title="eDIL Bibliography">Thes.</strong></span> ii 353.5
          
         (Hy. vi). 
a mesrugud . . . arnara fochund galir. 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Mon.Tall." title="eDIL Bibliography">Mon. Tall.</strong></span> 152.11

        . 
          dénaid oenbuidin . . . arnachab-accastar as tír, 

            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/BDD2" title="eDIL Bibliography">BDD²</strong></span> 435
          
        . 
          a sith do denam ar comad tresiti iad, 
            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/&#xC9;riu" title="eDIL Bibliography">Ériu</strong></span> viii

        . 
          ar gomadh tormach neirt don aird-ri, 
            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/BNn&#xC9;" title="eDIL Bibliography">BNnÉ</strong></span> 321.y
          
        .</p>
     
      <p class="result">

        . asrubart dia friu som

          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Ml." title="eDIL Bibliography">Ml.</strong></span> 102d3

      <p class="result">

          focairther ō Dil arnā rogonta nech ann
