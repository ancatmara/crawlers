<div class="row result"><div class="col-sm-3 result_headword"><h3 headword_id="34507">prap(p)</h3><span class="citation">Cite this: eDIL s.v. prap(p) or <a class="reference" href="34507">dil.ie/34507</a></span><br/><span class="revision">Last Revised:
                2013</span><br/><p style="margin-top: 4px" class="small">Forms:
                praipidi, prap, prap, prap, prap, prapchaillte</p></div><div class="col-sm-9 result_content">
   


    <p class="result">
      <span class="pos adj." style="color: #ff8000">adj.</span> (onomat.) <span class="trans">
        <span class="def">quick</span>, <span class="def">rapid</span>, <span class="def">sudden</span>
      </span>, gen. as pred. with cop. is 
prap a persannacht `<span class="quote">his character is changeable (?)</span>', 
        <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Tec.Corm." title="eDIL Bibliography">Tec. Corm.</strong></span> 
§ 33.6
       (<span class="trans">
        <span class="def">hasty</span>, <span class="def">abrupt</span>?</span>). 
        prap i comlond clíathcha in caemh, 

          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/BB" title="eDIL Bibliography">BB</strong></span> 53a35
        
      . 
        rapa phrapp in chomeirggi, 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/LL" title="eDIL Bibliography">LL</strong></span> 27a53
        
      . ger prap 
sain da bās ga buain | ar fās in air re haenuair <span class="ediltr">the crop springing up at once was being already reaped, quick (soon) though 
it was</span>, 
        <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/ACL" title="eDIL Bibliography">ACL</strong></span> iii 245 § 29
      . fāth is prap <span class="ediltr">the reason is quickly seen; 
obvious</span> (?), 
        <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/ZCP" title="eDIL Bibliography">ZCP</strong></span> vi 268.4
      . <span style="color: #ff8000">Compar.</span> with DE: feóil lenum 
doberdis dí combad praipiti no fhoirbredh <span class="ediltr">that she might grow 
faster</span>, 
        <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/C&#xF3;irAnm." title="eDIL Bibliography">Cóir Anm.</strong></span> 170
       = 
        combad prapiti no āsad, 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/LU" title="eDIL Bibliography">LU</strong></span> 4436
        
      . ni

fighting</span>, 
        <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/CCath." title="eDIL Bibliography">CCath.</strong></span> 6133
      . 
        praipidi, 
          3403
        
      . Rarely as attrib.: 
ruathar prap <span class="ediltr">a speedy rout</span>, 
        <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/&#xC9;riu" title="eDIL Bibliography">Ériu</strong></span> iv 226.17
      . iomad ngiall prap 
`<span class="quote">many swift captives</span>' (? <span class="ediltr">hostages ready at hand</span>), 
        <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/ITS" title="eDIL Bibliography">ITS</strong></span> v 56.17
      .</p>
  
  
    <p class="result">More often <span class="pos adv." style="color: #ff8000">adv.</span> <span class="trans">

      </span>: 
        
          prap ros rathaig
