<div class="row result"><div class="col-sm-3 result_headword"><h3 headword_id="1007">áilges</h3><span class="citation">Cite this: eDIL s.v. áilges or <a class="reference" href="1007">dil.ie/1007</a></span><br/><p style="margin-top: 4px" class="small">Forms:
                á-, ailgess, ailgis, ailgais, algis, algais, hālgese, algessi, algaisse, ailgeasa, t-álgas, áilgi[o]s, algiusa, ailciusa, alghusa, álghais</p></div><div class="col-sm-9 result_content">
  
  
    <p class="result">
      <span class="pos n" style="color: #ff8000">n</span> 
      <span class="itype &#x101;">ā</span>, <span class="gen f.">f.</span>, later (?) <span class="itype u">u</span> and <span class="itype o">o</span>, <span class="gen m.">m.</span> The meaning and earlier 
inflection are against analysis as deriv. of <span class="ref"><a class="normal" href="search?search_in=headword&amp;q=&#xE1;laic">álaic</a></span> (<span class="lbl">cf.</span> Desid. 
Gloss.) though the rhyming exx. (
        <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Metr.Dinds." title="eDIL Bibliography">Metr. Dinds.</strong></span> iii 62.107
       and 
bardic verse <span class="lbl">passim</span>) indicate long á-. The length-mark is not 
usual in early exx., and 
        <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Heldensage" title="eDIL Bibliography">Heldensage</strong></span> 69
      , on the basis 
of 
        <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Corm.Y" title="eDIL Bibliography">Corm. Y</strong></span> 62
      , takes as compd. of <span class="ref"><a class="normal" href="search?search_in=headword&amp;q=2 ail">2 ail</a></span> and <span class="ref"><a class="normal" href="search?search_in=headword&amp;q=geis">geis</a></span> `<span class="quote">Schimpf-Bitte</span>' (note that geis in general sense of <span class="ediltr">request</span> is poorly 
attested). Desid. Gloss. suggests compd. of <span class="ref"><a class="normal" href="search?search_in=headword&amp;q=&#xE1;il">áil</a></span> and <span class="ref"><a class="normal" href="search?search_in=headword&amp;q=geis">geis</a></span>. In later verse with unlen. -g-, as shown by the rhymes quoted <span class="lbl">infra</span>.</p>
