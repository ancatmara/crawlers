<div class="row result"><div class="col-sm-3 result_headword"><h3 headword_id="27356">imchían</h3><span class="citation">Cite this: eDIL s.v. imchían or <a class="reference" href="27356">dil.ie/27356</a></span><br/></div><div class="col-sm-9 result_content">
   
    <p class="result">
      <span class="pos adj" style="color: #ff8000">adj</span> 
      <span class="itype o,-&#x101;">o, ā</span> <span class="trans">
        <span class="def">very</span> <span class="def">far</span>, very <span class="def">distant</span>, very <span class="def">long</span> (of space or time)</span>.</p>
   
    
      <p class="result"> 
(<span class="edilb">a</span>) Of space : 
          a tírib imciana, 
            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/TBC-LL1" title="eDIL Bibliography">TBC-LL¹</strong></span> 926.
          
         edh imcian 
o chuan an mara suas `<span class="quote">a long distance up from the sea-haven</span>,' 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/CCath." title="eDIL Bibliography">CCath.</strong></span> 2875.
         tucustar grādh n-imcīan n-ēcmaisi 
do Fhrōech (i.e. though he was far away), 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/ZCP" title="eDIL Bibliography">ZCP</strong></span> xiii 167.16
        . 
batir ardda imchiana a da ṅgruad <span class="ediltr">very long</span> ?, 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/IrishTexts" title="eDIL Bibliography">Irish Texts</strong></span> 
i 33.7
        . síol gColla na gcreach n-imchian `<span class="quote">the far-raiding 
race of Colla</span>,' 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/TD" title="eDIL Bibliography">TD</strong></span> 13 § 43.
         is imchian ┐ ó chríochaibh imill 
na cruinne a luach súd `<span class="quote">procul et de ultimis finibus pretium 
eius</span>,' 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/TSh." title="eDIL Bibliography">TSh.</strong></span> 10691.
         as tír imchéin `<span class="quote">from a far country</span>,' 

          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Bedel" title="eDIL Bibliography">Proverbs</strong></span> xxv 25.
        
      </p>
    
  
  
    
      <p class="result"> 
(<span class="edilb">b</span>) Of time : 
          re hethadh fada imchéin d'aimsir, 
            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/&#xC9;riu" title="eDIL Bibliography">Ériu</strong></span> 
v 84.11
          
        . 
          ní himcian ro bói fer na foraire ann, 
            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Acall." title="eDIL Bibliography">Acall.</strong></span> 5157.
          
         

          hi cinn ree imcéine, 
            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/BNn&#xC9;" title="eDIL Bibliography">BNnÉ</strong></span> 83 § 165.
          
         
          cidh nach imchian 
atúsa in bhar neccmais, 
            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Leb.Gab.i" title="eDIL Bibliography">Leb. Gab.(i)</strong></span> 28.12
          
        . 
          dá dtabhair Dia 
athaidh fhada ┐ aimsir imchian ré déanamh aithrighe, 

            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/TSh." title="eDIL Bibliography">TSh.</strong></span> 2294.
          
         
          aithnidh damhsa agus do féin | anos re haimsir 
n-imchéin, 
            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/TD" title="eDIL Bibliography">TD</strong></span> 18 § 7.
          
         <span class="trans">
          <span class="def">long-enduring</span>, <span class="def">lasting</span> ? :</span> iss anfola 
immarcraidh ┐ díth deg-dáini ┐ is argni imcíana `<span class="quote">long-during slaughters</span>,' 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/TTr." title="eDIL Bibliography">TTr.</strong></span> 1613.
         In a cheville : lecht imchían 
`<span class="quote">a lasting grave</span>,' 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Imr.Brain" title="eDIL Bibliography">Imr. Brain</strong></span> § 55
         (or perh. under (a) <span class="ediltr">a far-extending grave</span>).</p>
    
  
  
    <p class="result"> 
As <span class="pos adv." style="color: #ff8000">adv.</span> 
        doronsat imairecc aingidi co himcian friu, 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/StairErc." title="eDIL Bibliography">Stair Erc.</strong></span> 35.
        
      
    </p>
  
  
    <p class="result"> 
As <span class="pos subst." style="color: #ff8000">subst.</span> <span class="itype &#x101;">ā</span>, <span class="gen f.">f.</span> also <span class="itype o">o</span>, <span class="gen m.">m.</span> <span class="trans">
        <i>a</i> <span class="def">great</span> <span class="def">distance</span> (of space or time), 
<i>a</i> <span class="def">distant</span> <span class="def">place</span>:</span> ni raibi d' imcian crichi etarru `<span class="quote">there was 
not such a distance of land between them</span>,' 
        <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Laws" title="eDIL Bibliography">Laws</strong></span> iii 406.8
      . 

        na heirig a n-imcian úaim, 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Acall." title="eDIL Bibliography">Acall.</strong></span> 6989.
        
       
        atcondairc ... ard-oilein in imchein uaidh, 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Leb.Gab.i" title="eDIL Bibliography">Leb. Gab.(i)</strong></span> 242.20
        
      . 
        is fearr cara a 
ccomhfhogus ná dearbhrathair a n-imchían, 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Bedel" title="eDIL Bibliography">Proverbs</strong></span> xxvii 
10
        
      . do-ní amharc an éaga i n-imchian uatha <span class="ediltr">looking 
at death far away</span>, 
        <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/TSh." title="eDIL Bibliography">TSh.</strong></span> 743.
       
        ar deóraidheacht i n-imchéin, 

          3449
        
      .</p>
  
  
    <p class="result"> 
In the foll. exx. the `c' seems to be eclipsed (<span class="lbl">cf.</span> Mod. Ir. 

        imgcéin, 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/DD&#xE1;na" title="eDIL Bibliography">DDána</strong></span> 66 § 9
        
      , 
        imigcéin, 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/S.Cl&#xE1;rach" title="eDIL Bibliography">Amhráin Sheagháin Chláraigh Mhic Dhomhnaill</strong></span> (ed. Ó Duinnín) l. 268
        
      ), which 
O'Brien explains as a contamination of i n-imchian and 
i gcéin (
        <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/&#xC9;riu" title="eDIL Bibliography">Ériu</strong></span> xi 170
      )). 
        da gai trí Choncobar féin | 's a trí tri 
Brod ni imcén, 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/IT" title="eDIL Bibliography">IT</strong></span> iii 504.8

        rop imgen ra Conchobar ┐ ra

          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/CRR" title="eDIL Bibliography">CRR</strong></span> § 26.
        
       
        co clos fo 
imcen na crich, 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/IT" title="eDIL Bibliography">IT</strong></span> ii2 125.89
        
      . dar na fetar dul cen snam 
no cen ethar, no cen imcein criche `<span class="quote">without a great round 
by land</span>,' 
        <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Laws" title="eDIL Bibliography">Laws</strong></span> iii 318.7 Comm.
