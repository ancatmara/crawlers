      <span class="pos n" style="color: #ff8000">n</span> 
      <span class="itype o">o</span>, <span class="gen m.">m.</span> <span class="pos vn." style="color: #ff8000">vn.</span> formation from *to-air-(fo ?)-cuir-. turchar 
        torchar, 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/IGT" title="eDIL Bibliography">IGT</strong></span> Decl. § 11 (54.4)

(<span class="edilb">a</span>) Orig. perh. <span class="trans">act of <span class="def">throwing</span>, <span class="def">placing</span>:</span> 
          taurthait .i.

         (<span class="lbl">cf.</span> 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Corm.Y" title="eDIL Bibliography">Corm. Y</strong></span> 1229
        ). for lassad co léir 
... | ola ┐ cheir ... | iarna terchur fri dídnad | cen herchur, 
cen airdíbdad <span class="ediltr">placed there for consolation</span> (?), 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/SR" title="eDIL Bibliography">SR</strong></span> 4371.
        
      </p>
    
  
  
    
      <p class="result"> 
(<span class="edilb">b</span>) <span class="trans">
          <span class="def">chance</span>, <span class="def">hap</span>, <span class="def">accident</span>:</span> teacmoc .i. toice, no turchar,

          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/RC" title="eDIL Bibliography">RC</strong></span> xxv 
250.17
         (
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/LU" title="eDIL Bibliography">LU</strong></span> 2701
        ). na ro aiplium o thircur (thirchur, <i>v.l.</i>) '<span class="quote">lest we die by a chance</span>', 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/ITS" title="eDIL Bibliography">ITS</strong></span> xxxiv 66 § 31
        , cf. ne forte moriamur, 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Bedel" title="eDIL Bibliography">Gen.</strong></span> 3, 3
        .</p>
