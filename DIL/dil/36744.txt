        <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/GOI" title="eDIL Bibliography">GOI</strong></span> § 35

        § 883

an explanatory word, phrase or clause <span class="trans">that is to <span class="def">say</span>, <span class="def">namely</span>,

      </span>, folld. by abs. (deut.) form of vb. Rare in Wb., 
does not occur in Sg., but freq. in Ml. Often preceded by .i.

pres. indic. of copula this is omitted. Other parts of copula 
remain: 
        .i. sech is óen spirut fotdáli,
