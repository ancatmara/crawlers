<div class="row result"><div class="col-sm-3 result_headword"><h3 headword_id="33202">1 no</h3><span class="citation">Cite this: eDIL s.v. 1 no or <a class="reference" href="33202">dil.ie/33202</a></span><br/><p style="margin-top: 4px" class="small">Forms:
                nu, no, no</p></div><div class="col-sm-9 result_content">
   
    <p class="result">
      <span class="pos preverb" style="color: #ff8000">preverb</span>.</p>
   
    <p class="result"> 
In O.Ir. used only with simple vb. in follg. cases: (<span class="edilb">a</span>) regularly in impf. ind., impf. subj. and cond., when they are not 
preceded by any particle requiring the conjunct form of the 
vb.; this construction continues through Mid.Ir.; in Mod.Ir. 
do is used. (<span class="edilb">b</span>) in 1 and 2 s. and pl. of pres. ind., pres. subj. 
and fut. when they are used relatively. (<span class="edilb">c</span>) with all tenses of 
ind. and subj. to infix <span style="color: #ff8000">rel.</span> n-. (<span class="edilb">d</span>) with all parts of the vb., 
including the imp., to infix a pron.</p>
   
    <p class="result"> 
Occas. written nu in O. and Mid.Ir.; 
        nuggabad, 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Thes." title="eDIL Bibliography">Thes.</strong></span> ii 
242.7
        
       (
        <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Ardm." title="eDIL Bibliography">Ardm.</strong></span> 18b1
      ); other exx. below.</p>
   
    <p class="result"> 
Generally lenites the initial of the vb. in rel. sentences where 
there is no infixed pron.</p>
