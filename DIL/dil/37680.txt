          ba sé sin dano in siriti on ainmnigther

            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/LU" title="eDIL Bibliography">LU</strong></span> 9008
          
         (
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/FB" title="eDIL Bibliography">FB</strong></span> 75
        ) (<span class="lbl">cf.</span> 
          Belach Mune na 
sirride, 
            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/RC" title="eDIL Bibliography">RC</strong></span> xviii 163.27
          
         (Tig.)). 
          is de atberthe in siriti de ara 
met no delbad i n-ilrechtaib, 
            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/LU" title="eDIL Bibliography">LU</strong></span> 9009
          
         (
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/FB" title="eDIL Bibliography">FB</strong></span> 75
        ). Often qualified or accompanied by <span class="ref"><a class="normal" href="search?search_in=headword&amp;q=s&#xED;abartha">síabartha</a></span>, <span class="lbl">q.v.</span>:

            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/TBC-LL1" title="eDIL Bibliography">TBC-LL¹</strong></span> 1911

            3794
          
        . 
          maith, a ṡiriti, a ṡiabairthe, 
            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/RC" title="eDIL Bibliography">RC</strong></span> xiv 408 § 19
          
        . </p>
    
  
  
    
      <p class="result"> 
(<span class="edilb">b</span>) usually a mildly contemptuous term applied to Cú 
Culainn by both friends and enemies (<span class="lbl">cf.</span> <span class="ref"><a class="normal" href="search?search_in=headword&amp;q=r&#xED;astartha(e)">ríastartha(e)</a></span>). <span class="trans">
