    <p class="result"> 
<span class="trans">Used in connection with a space of time, <span class="def">consecutively</span> ?, or 
perhaps, <span class="def">in all</span>, <span class="def">altogether</span>:</span> 
        secht ṁbliadna lana mole | ... | 
ó gab Ioseph gloir garta | co aimsir na mórgorta, 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/SR" title="eDIL Bibliography">SR</strong></span> 3381.
        
       

        tri bliadhna deg immaille | rofaemadh Niall caem Caille, 

          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/ToddLect." title="eDIL Bibliography">Todd Lect.</strong></span> iii 422.5
        
      . 
        ix mbliadna fichet malle | ... | ... | i 
rríge os Banba bratrúaid, 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/LL" title="eDIL Bibliography">LL</strong></span> 127b7
        
      . 
        naoi fichit bliadain 
mole ... ba he saogal Moninde, 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/AFM" title="eDIL Bibliography">AFM</strong></span> i 168.16
        
      .</p>
  
  
    <p class="result"> 
Combined with fri (re), la, forming a prep. phrase.</p>
