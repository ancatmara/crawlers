      <span class="pos n" style="color: #ff8000">n</span> (crott + ball). Also crot(f)al(l). crobhtall (<span class="lbl">sic</span>) 
(crotall, <span class="lbl">v.l.</span>) <span class="gen m.">m.</span>, 
        <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/IGT" title="eDIL Bibliography">IGT</strong></span> Decl.§ 11 (53.z.)
      
    </p>

          <span class="def">decrepitude</span>,

        </span>: ni coimtíg crotball tria cli (of old man), 

          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/ZCP" title="eDIL Bibliography">ZCP</strong></span> xiii 266.4
        . 
          crotboll sádaile ┐ senordachta, 
            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Acall." title="eDIL Bibliography">Acall.</strong></span> 3200
          
        . 

          crotfall crine, 
            4441
          
        . 
          do ghabh crotbhall críne / an bogcrann, 

            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/DD&#xE1;na" title="eDIL Bibliography">DDána</strong></span> 41.14
          
        .</p>
    
  
  
    
      <p class="result"> 
(<span class="edilb">b</span>) <span class="trans">

          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/BCC" title="eDIL Bibliography">BCC</strong></span> 440.7
