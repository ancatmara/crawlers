<div class="row result"><div class="col-sm-3 result_headword"><h3 headword_id="19799">1 éim, ém</h3><span class="citation">Cite this: eDIL s.v. 1 éim, ém or <a class="reference" href="19799">dil.ie/19799</a></span><br/><span class="revision">Last Revised:

    <p class="result">in Glosses also ǽm, æm. Apparently a 
by-form of <span class="ref"><a class="normal" href="search?search_in=headword&amp;q=1 &#xE1;m">1 ám</a></span> (<span class="lbl">q.v.</span>) of which it is often a variant reading. Used as a <span class="pos particle" style="color: #ff8000">particle</span> of asseveration or affirmation, 
usually emphasising the word or clause which it follows, 
but often merely expletive ; <span class="trans">
        <span class="def">truly</span>, <span class="def">indeed</span>, <span class="def">in sooth</span>, <span class="def">then</span>
      </span>.</p>
