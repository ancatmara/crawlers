and substantive vb. (but:

          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/ML2" title="eDIL Bibliography">ML²</strong></span> 1420

(but not in Wb.), especially before pronouns é, í, ed: Folld. 
by dependent form of vb. and neg. particle and by Class C 
forms of infixed pron. Later Mid. and Mod. Ir.: an.</p>
