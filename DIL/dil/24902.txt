<div class="row result"><div class="col-sm-3 result_headword"><h3 headword_id="24902">fuirmiud</h3><span class="citation">Cite this: eDIL s.v. fuirmiud or <a class="reference" href="24902">dil.ie/24902</a></span><br/><p style="margin-top: 4px" class="small">Forms:

    <p class="result">
      <span class="pos n" style="color: #ff8000">n</span>  <span class="itype u">u</span>, <span class="itype o">o</span>, <span class="gen m.">m.</span> (<span class="pos vn." style="color: #ff8000">vn.</span> of <span class="ref"><a class="normal" href="search?search_in=headword&amp;q=fo-ruimi">fo-ruimi</a></span>) The m is occasionally 
shown lenited ; O'Cl. distinguishes between fuirmeadh .i. 
toirneamh ; fuirmeadh .i. foreimniughad no siubhal, and 
fuirmheadh .i. cur (see below) but there is no reason to 
doubt that all these represent the same word.</p>
