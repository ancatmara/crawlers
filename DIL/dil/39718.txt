      <span class="pos adj" style="color: #ff8000">adj</span> 
      <span class="itype io,-i&#x101;">io, iā</span> O. Ir. toraisse, taraisse, the form tairis(s)e 
being due to the influence of <span class="ref"><a class="normal" href="search?search_in=headword&amp;q=tairissem">tairissem</a></span> ( 
        <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/PedersenVgl.Gr." title="eDIL Bibliography">Pedersen Vgl. Gr.</strong></span> ii 628
      -
        9
      ). 
From *to-ro-finn-, orig. meaning `<span class="quote">erkundet</span>' (<span class="lbl">ib.</span>). <span class="lbl">Cf.</span> 
however 
        <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Thes." title="eDIL Bibliography">Thes.</strong></span> i 720

<span class="edilb">I</span> In O. and Mid. Ir.</p>
     
      <p class="result">(<span class="edilb">a</span>) <span class="trans">
          <span class="def">fitting</span>, <span class="def">suitable</span>, <span class="def">meet</span>:</span> 
          idon <span class="frn ">ab eo 
quod est idoneus</span> .i. tairisi, 
            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Corm.Y" title="eDIL Bibliography">Corm. Y</strong></span> 78
          
        . it taraissai gl.

          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Ml." title="eDIL Bibliography">Ml.</strong></span> 42b15

`<span class="quote">he deems it meet</span>,' 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Wb." title="eDIL Bibliography">Wb.</strong></span> 15a15

ailli a almsine `<span class="quote">everyone deems it proper</span>,' 
          16d2
        .</p>
     
      
        <p class="result">(<span class="edilb">b</span>) <span class="trans">
