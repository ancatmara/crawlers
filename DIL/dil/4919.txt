<div class="row result"><div class="col-sm-3 result_headword"><h3 headword_id="4919">at-roí</h3><span class="citation">Cite this: eDIL s.v. at-roí or <a class="reference" href="4919">dil.ie/4919</a></span><br/></div><div class="col-sm-9 result_content">
  
  
    <p class="result">
      <span class="pos v" style="color: #ff8000">v</span> expld. by 
        <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/PedersenVgl.Gr." title="eDIL Bibliography">Pedersen Vgl. Gr.</strong></span> ii 636
       as from *ess-ro-so- with inf. 
pron. <span class="trans">
        <span class="def">failed</span> (to do or get someth.)</span>. `Orig. the person who 
failed was the object, what he failed to achieve was the 
subject; <span class="lbl">e.g.</span> a-t-roi a n-guin "<span class="ediltr">their killing turned him 
away</span>'' "<span class="ediltr">he failed to kill them</span>'' (
        <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Hib.Min." title="eDIL Bibliography">Hib. Min.</strong></span> 70.10
      )', 
        <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/LewisandPed." title="eDIL Bibliography">Lewis and Ped.</strong></span> 398
      . Usage however fluctuates somewhat in the 
exx., and it is not certain that the verb contains the root 
*so- or that it is connected with éraid (see <span class="ref"><a class="normal" href="search?search_in=headword&amp;q=as-ren">as-ren</a></span>, <span class="ref"><a class="normal" href="search?search_in=headword&amp;q=&#xE9;raid">éraid</a></span>, 
<span class="ref"><a class="normal" href="search?search_in=headword&amp;q=as-so&#xED;">as-soí</a></span>). rabāi . . . orci i seilb charat Cairpri . . . atrōe 
Coirpri ūad <span class="ediltr">C. could not get it (the dog) from him</span>, 
        <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Corm.Y" title="eDIL Bibliography">Corm. Y</strong></span> 
883
      . tíagait úad íarum. ata-roí a n-astod <span class="ediltr">they could not</span> 
<span class="ediltr">stop them</span>, 
        <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/BDD2" title="eDIL Bibliography">BDD²</strong></span> 308
       (`<span class="quote">das Festhalten wendete sie ab, sie 
konnten nicht zurückgehalten werden</span>', Ped. <span class="lbl">loc. cit.</span>). at-roaside díchleith n-aire ┐ do-beir lán a chúaich de usci as 
<span class="ediltr">it could not be concealed from him</span> (a well), 
        <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/BDD2" title="eDIL Bibliography">BDD²</strong></span> 1468
       = 

        atroasidé a dícleth, 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/LU" title="eDIL Bibliography">LU</strong></span> 7937
        
      . 
        ad-raī a nguin la toescad 
in muilinn, 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Fing.R." title="eDIL Bibliography">Fing. R.</strong></span> 879
        
       (see above).</p>
  
  
    <p class="result"> 
In follg. application of inf. pron. less clear: tuirsech mo 
menma. Addro luamain ┐ addagin eónu aili <span class="ediltr">I could not fly</span>, 

        <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Imr.Brain" title="eDIL Bibliography">Imr. Brain</strong></span> ii 292.31
       (see 
        <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Celtica" title="eDIL Bibliography">Celtica</strong></span> v 139
      ). doluid críne form 
assennath ┐ addró imthecht (forémed, <span class="lbl">v.l.</span>) <span class="ediltr">I was unable to 
walk</span>, 
        287.30
      .</p>
  
  
    <p class="result"> 
With subj. indicated by verbal ending: 
        atrōiset Luiguir 
co ndesetar impi, 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Otia" title="eDIL Bibliography">Otia</strong></span> ii 87 § 8
        
       = 
        adraiset Ligur, 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/BB" title="eDIL Bibliography">BB</strong></span> 135a18
        
       
<span class="ediltr">they were unable to cross the Loire</span>. With various <span class="voice pass.">pass.</span> 
endings: ? aradroagas a thedachtsom isin chath <span class="ediltr">had been 
prevented</span>, 
        <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/TBC-I1" title="eDIL Bibliography">TBC-I¹</strong></span> 3619
      . forémdes . . . o Chobthach dul issin 
tech <span class="ediltr">C. was unable to enter the house</span>, 
        <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/LL" title="eDIL Bibliography">LL</strong></span> 270a12
       (atroas <span class="lbl">v.l.</span>, 

        <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/ZCP" title="eDIL Bibliography">ZCP</strong></span> iii 7 § 24
      ). at-rós a thairmesc <span class="ediltr">he could not be prevented</span>, 

        <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Fing.R." title="eDIL Bibliography">Fing. R.</strong></span> 14
      . at-roas techt ar in orggain <span class="ediltr">they were unable</span>, 

        397
      . 
        na conétat comaitecht (attaroet comhaidecht <span class="lbl">v.l.</span>), 

          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/RC" title="eDIL Bibliography">RC</strong></span> xxi 376.9
        
      . attroithe a rrím <span class="ediltr">they could not be counted</span>, 

        <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/LL" title="eDIL Bibliography">LL</strong></span> 290a18
       (
        <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/RC" title="eDIL Bibliography">RC</strong></span> xiii 448.x
      ). Without inf. pron.: gegnaitsom 
banb bec díb, asrous iarom a brith día doud <span class="ediltr">they were unable 
to bring it to be roasted</span>, 
        <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/LU" title="eDIL Bibliography">LU</strong></span> 1742
      .</p>
  
  
    <p class="result">

        romgobsa mo gesa ule anocht, ol 
C., hua ro-essa indarboe in tríar sin,

        Fiachra Cassán febda in

      . ? atroroi

        <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/ZCP" title="eDIL Bibliography">ZCP</strong></span> xii 402.y
      . 
        ataforroi cath comortai.

          403.9
        
      . Perh. also: 
        atroebaid Gaidel co mbúaid / 
dula dó i ndegaid int slúaig / uair ba faith, fri glea scél /

          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/SR" title="eDIL Bibliography">SR</strong></span> 3997

          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/LB" title="eDIL Bibliography">LB</strong></span> 118b52
