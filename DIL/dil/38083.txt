          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/OGr.Cat." title="eDIL Bibliography">O'Gr. Cat.</strong></span>

          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/D&#xE1;nD&#xE9;" title="eDIL Bibliography">Dán Dé</strong></span> xxiii 33

            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/RC" title="eDIL Bibliography">RC</strong></span>

for any other care</span>', 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Studies" title="eDIL Bibliography">Studies</strong></span> 1919, 258 § 23

fuirri, 
            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/ZCP" title="eDIL Bibliography">ZCP</strong></span> vi 27.3
