          <span class="def">happening</span>, <span class="def">chancing</span>, <span class="def">occurring</span>:</span>

            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/AFM" title="eDIL Bibliography">AFM</strong></span> iii 236.4
          
        .

`<span class="quote">to fall there by chance</span>' (<span class="lbl">i.e.</span> the coast of England),

          teagmháil, teacht a gcomhdhail a n-airrcis,
