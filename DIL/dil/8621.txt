<div class="row result"><div class="col-sm-3 result_headword"><h3 headword_id="8621">cenmothá</h3><span class="citation">Cite this: eDIL s.v. cenmothá or <a class="reference" href="8621">dil.ie/8621</a></span><br/><p style="margin-top: 4px" class="small">Forms:
                cenmithá, cenm, cinmothā, genmothá, cenmothát, cenmibí, cenmobi, cenmorobai, cenmo, cenmothá, cenmothá, cenmotha, genmothá, -át, cenmóthát</p></div><div class="col-sm-9 result_content">
   
    <p class="result">Compd. of cen and -tá (see <span class="ref"><a class="normal" href="search?search_in=headword&amp;q=att&#xE1;">attá</a></span>) used as <span class="pos prep." style="color: #ff8000">prep.</span> 
and <span class="pos conjunction." style="color: #ff8000">conjunction.</span> See 
        <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/GOI" title="eDIL Bibliography">GOI</strong></span> § 775
      . In O. Ir. 
usually cenmithá (
        cenmathá, 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Wb." title="eDIL Bibliography">Wb.</strong></span> 33a4
        
      , 
        <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Sg." title="eDIL Bibliography">Sg.</strong></span> 56b13
      , 
        71b17
      ; 

        cenm, 
          201b18
        
      ). 
        cinmothā, 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/&#xC9;riu" title="eDIL Bibliography">Ériu</strong></span> i 124.10
        
      . In later lang. 
also genmothá. With <span class="number pl.">pl.</span> ending cenmothát (cenmthat disyll., 

        <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/SR" title="eDIL Bibliography">SR</strong></span> 399
      ). Rarely with other forms of subst. vb.: 
        cenmibí, 

          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/&#xC9;riu" title="eDIL Bibliography">Ériu</strong></span> vii 148 § 8
        
      . 
        cenmobi, 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/YBL" title="eDIL Bibliography">YBL</strong></span> 140b1
        
      . 
        cenmorobai, 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/&#xC9;riu" title="eDIL Bibliography">Ériu</strong></span> 
vii 164.11
        
      . 
        
          cenmo bui, 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/TFerbe" title="eDIL Bibliography">TFerbe</strong></span> 27
        
      . The general rule for all 
periods of the language is that the form cenmothá <span class="lbl">etc.</span> 
is folld. by accus. (<span class="number sg.">sg.</span> and <span class="number pl.">pl.</span>), exceptions being rare. <span class="lbl">Cf.</span> 

        
          cenmothá na .uii. claidseo, 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Sench.M&#xF3;r" title="eDIL Bibliography">SM Facs.</strong></span> 18a8
        
       (emended to 

        cladu-so, 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/&#xC9;riu" title="eDIL Bibliography">Ériu</strong></span> xvii 72 § 14
        
      ). 
        
          cenmotha dias, 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/RC" title="eDIL Bibliography">RC</strong></span> xxiv 200.15
        
      . 

        genmothá somh <span class="ediltr">besides him</span>, 
        <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/AFM" title="eDIL Bibliography">AFM</strong></span> iv 864.1
      . See  

        <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/GOI" title="eDIL Bibliography">GOI</strong></span> Translator's Notes no. 169
      . The <span class="number pl.">pl.</span> form in -át is folld. by 
the nom. pl. (early exx. being 
        <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/&#xC9;riu" title="eDIL Bibliography">Ériu</strong></span> xiii 18.13
      , 
        <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/SR" title="eDIL Bibliography">SR</strong></span> 399
      , 
        3421
      ) 
or stands by itself: cenmóthát <span class="ediltr">besides them</span>, 
        <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/AFM" title="eDIL Bibliography">AFM</strong></span> iii 126.9
      . 
See also 
        <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/ZCP" title="eDIL Bibliography">ZCP</strong></span> xxviii 125
      -
        6
      .</p>
