and <span class="case n">n</span>
      <span class="number p.">p.</span>
       -e. <span class="pos Vn." style="color: #ff8000">Vn.</span> of <span class="ref"><a class="normal" href="search?search_in=headword&amp;q=2 ad-cumaing">2 ad-cumaing</a></span>. See also under <span class="ref"><a class="normal" href="search?search_in=headword&amp;q=tacmang">tacmang</a></span>.</p>
