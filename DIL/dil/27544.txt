<div class="row result"><div class="col-sm-3 result_headword"><h3 headword_id="27544">imḟolang, imḟulang</h3><span class="citation">Cite this: eDIL s.v. imḟolang, imḟulang or <a class="reference" href="27544">dil.ie/27544</a></span><br/><p style="margin-top: 4px" class="small">Forms:
                imfhulaing, imfulaing</p></div><div class="col-sm-9 result_content">
   
    <p class="result">
      <span class="pos n" style="color: #ff8000">n</span> 
      <span class="itype o">o</span>, <span class="gen m.">m.</span> (perh. orig. <span class="itype o">o</span>, <span class="gen n.">n.</span> See 
        <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Cr&#xED;thG." title="eDIL Bibliography">Críth G.</strong></span> 
<span class="lbl">s.v.</span> folog
      ). <span class="pos vn." style="color: #ff8000">vn.</span> of <span class="ref"><a class="normal" href="search?search_in=headword&amp;q=imm-foilngi">imm-foilngi</a></span>.</p>
   
    
      <p class="result">(<span class="edilb">a</span>) <span class="trans">the act of <span class="def">causing</span>, 
<span class="def">effecting</span>, <span class="def">accomplishing</span> ; <span class="def">cause</span>:</span> imfolang nach gnima 
`<span class="quote">the effecting of any deed</span>,' 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Ml." title="eDIL Bibliography">Ml.</strong></span> 80d2
        . dind imfolung, 
gl. de effectu, 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Ml." title="eDIL Bibliography">Ml.</strong></span> 24c17
        . oc immfolung ind óir promthi 
`<span class="quote">making proved gold</span>,' 
          38d18
        . 
          do immofoluṅg déesse duitsiu, 

            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Acr." title="eDIL Bibliography">Acr.</strong></span> 6c3
          
         (
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Thes." title="eDIL Bibliography">Thes.</strong></span> ii 3.29
        ). ma dogabthar chetamus a fít is 
imfolang galir són `<span class="quote">if a man's allowance be suddenly diminished, that will cause sickness</span>,' 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Mon.Tall." title="eDIL Bibliography">Mon. Tall.</strong></span> 152.8
        . 
          fodáilter 
dona tuathaib fri himfhulang n-ícce, 
            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/PH" title="eDIL Bibliography">PH</strong></span> 6358.
          
         tria imfhulang in aicnid spirutalda `<span class="quote">per effectum spiritualis potentiae</span>,' 
          6747
        .</p>
     
      <p class="result"> 
Here seems to mean <span class="trans">
          <span class="def">effect</span>:</span> imfolang a chomairle son 
`<span class="quote">the effect of his council</span>,' 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Ml." title="eDIL Bibliography">Ml.</strong></span> 30d22a
        .</p>
    
  
  
    
      <p class="result"> 
(<span class="edilb">b</span>) <span class="trans">
          <i>the act of</i> <span class="def">sustaining</span>, <span class="def">supporting</span> ; <i>means of</i> <span class="def">support</span> :</span> 
fri imfolang ar mbethad `<span class="quote">to support our life</span>,' 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Ml." title="eDIL Bibliography">Ml.</strong></span> 20d5
        . 

          batir banbrugaid son ┐ batar maithi do imfulung na fían, 

            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Corm.Y" title="eDIL Bibliography">Corm. Y</strong></span> 1018
          
         (
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Anecd." title="eDIL Bibliography">Anecd.</strong></span> iv 87.12
        ). 
          ata lanamuin i tuaith 
dlegtai a notrus .i. a nimfulung, 
            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/&#xC9;riu" title="eDIL Bibliography">Ériu</strong></span> xii 10 § 9.
          
         
          d'imfulang 
a mathar ocus a n-athar, 
            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Laws" title="eDIL Bibliography">Laws</strong></span> i 138.18 Comm.
          
         
          imfulang 
na cetmuintire fri re tri mbliadan, 
            232.22
          
        . (gl. 
          folach cetmuintire, 
            230.24
          
        ). nocha nindlightuch do ma cunic a 
imfulung (of receiving a large supply of stock), 
          ii 226.9 
Comm.
         atait secht n-imfuiling, no sechta imfuilingter isin 
tuaith ┐ is doilig a n-imfulang `<span class="quote">seven cases of maintenance</span>,' 

          v 312.6 Comm.
         bo bis ac imḟulang a cisa do fhlaith no do 
eclais `<span class="quote">a cow which pays</span> (<span class="lbl">viz.</span>, <span class="ediltr">serves as payment of) his rent 
to the chief</span>,' 
          260.12 Comm.
         
          do gebhdis a sasadh do ghrés 
gan saothar ndaonna aga niomḟulang, 
            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Mart.Don." title="eDIL Bibliography">Mart. Don.</strong></span> 102.16
          
        . 

          ic a nertad ┐ ic a n-imfhulang co riachtatar sruth nEordanen, 

            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/LB" title="eDIL Bibliography">LB</strong></span> 123b39
          
        . 
          go ndarubdatar féithe do chride ... connach 
fail féith itir ic a immuluṅg, 
            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/TBC-LL1" title="eDIL Bibliography">TBC-LL¹</strong></span> 4387.
          
         
          is e connic ind uli 
dúl aicside ┐ nemaicside do airitin ┐ do imfulang, 
            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Alex." title="eDIL Bibliography">Alex.</strong></span> 451.
          
         

          a n-imfhulang ┐ a gcongbāil suass a mbethaidh ┐ a n-edaigib, 

            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Fl.Earls" title="eDIL Bibliography">Fl. Earls</strong></span> 180.18
          
        . go ffuil a uile riachtanuss ┐ imfulang ann 
badhdēin `<span class="quote">all its necessaries and support are supplied by 
itself</span>,' 
          182.7
        . 
          diarraidh ... choblaigh go n-iomfhulancc lóin 
┐ ordanais móir do chor co hErinn, 
            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/AFM" title="eDIL Bibliography">AFM</strong></span> v 1728.x
          
        .</p>
     
      <p class="result"> 
<span class="case Gen.">Gen.</span> used attributively, <span class="trans">
          <span class="def">enduring</span>, <span class="def">strong</span>:</span> 
          da tharb ... 
ua chuing adbail imfhulaing
          , 
            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/TTebe" title="eDIL Bibliography">TTebe</strong></span> 212
          
        . 
          co slabradaib

            465
          
        . 
          tre eirr imchail imfhulaing ... chath-sceith, 
            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/MR" title="eDIL Bibliography">MR</strong></span> 266.9
          
        .</p>
    
  
  
    
      <p class="result"> 
(<span class="edilb">c</span>) <span class="trans">the act of <span class="def">permitting</span>, <span class="def">allowing</span>:</span> is socar duna tuathaib 
imḟulung na ndaine co cintaibh accu tall isin tuaith `<span class="quote">to 
suffer the people with crimes among them in the territory</span>,' 

          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Laws" title="eDIL Bibliography">Laws</strong></span> v 30.3 Comm.
        
      </p>
    
  
  
    
      <p class="result"> 
(<span class="edilb">d</span>) <span class="trans">the act of <span class="def">carrying</span>, <span class="def">removing</span>:</span> cethrar aca imfhulong 
`<span class="quote">four carrying him</span>,' 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Ir.Review" title="eDIL Bibliography">Ir. Review</strong></span> 1912, 250 § 12
        . 
          masa 
cunntubairteach bāis an t-othair nocha ndleagar a imfhulaing co haithle na naomaighi, 
            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/OCurry" title="eDIL Bibliography">O'Curry</strong></span> 305
          
         (&lt; 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/H3.18" title="eDIL Bibliography">H 3.18</strong></span>, p. 167
        ). 

          baegal a imulung ria noemaid manar foccail liaig, 
            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/OD." title="eDIL Bibliography">O'D.</strong></span> 
1666
          
         (&lt;
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/H5.15" title="eDIL Bibliography">H 5.15</strong></span>, p.24b
