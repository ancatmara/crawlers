<div class="row result"><div class="col-sm-3 result_headword"><h3 headword_id="25996">2 glas</h3><span class="citation">Cite this: eDIL s.v. 2 glas or <a class="reference" href="25996">dil.ie/25996</a></span><br/><span class="revision">Last Revised:
                2013</span><br/><p style="margin-top: 4px" class="small">Forms:

    <p class="result">
      <span class="itype o,&#x101;">o,ā</span> <span class="pos adj." style="color: #ff8000">adj.</span> <span class="trans">descriptive of various <span class="def">shades</span> of <span class="def">light</span> <span class="def">green</span> and 
<span class="def">blue</span>, <span class="def">passing</span> from <span class="def">grass-green</span> to <span class="def">grey</span>
      </span>, opposed on the one 
hand to uaine <span class="ediltr">green</span> and on the other to gorm <span class="ediltr">blue</span>. It appears 
in the list of colours given as names to the winds in 
        <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/SR" title="eDIL Bibliography">SR</strong></span> 58 
      
      <span class="lbl">seq.</span>
