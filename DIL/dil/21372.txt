    <p class="result">
      <span class="pos n" style="color: #ff8000">n</span>  a word of doubtful meaning, expressing <span class="trans">
        <span class="def">woe</span>, <span class="def">calamity</span>, 
<span class="def">ill</span> <span class="def">omen</span>
      </span>.</p>
   
     

      <p class="result">(<span class="edilb">a</span>) acc. to Cormac's glossary the name given 
to a rod of yew, kept in pagan graveyards and used for 
measuring corpses and graves: ocus ba huath fri cāch a 
gabāil inda lāim, ┐ cach rēt ba adētchidhe la cāch dobentai 
frie nō dobertis trie ogham indti, <span class="frn ">unde in proverbium 
uenit</span> "fé fris," amail is n-adētc[h]ide in flesc <span class="frn ">cui nomen</span> fē, 
<span class="frn ">sic et alia res cui comparatur</span>
         <span class="ediltr">and everyone feared to handle
