      <p class="result"> 
<span class="edilb">II</span> As legal term,</p>
     
      
        <p class="result">(<span class="edilb">a</span>) <span class="trans">of persons, <span class="def">worthy</span>, <span class="def">trustworthy</span>, 
<span class="def">qualified</span> to <span class="def">perform</span> certain <span class="def">legal functions</span> (e.g. to <span class="def">give 
evidence</span>) :</span> fiadna innraice `<span class="quote">worthy witnesses</span>,' 
            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Laws" title="eDIL Bibliography">Laws</strong></span> v 368.2
          . 
With follg. gen. : rucus ba n-inraicc noilich fer ad-gair 
`<span class="quote">ich entschied, der Mann, der rechtlich belangt sei des Eides 
würdig</span>,' 
            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/ZCP" title="eDIL Bibliography">ZCP</strong></span> xv 361 § 46.
           is tualaing som forgell for cach 
recht, acht a da comgrad d'inraicaib `<span class="quote">except the two orders 
who are of equal standing with himself</span>,' 
            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Laws" title="eDIL Bibliography">Laws</strong></span> i 78.20
