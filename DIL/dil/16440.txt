<div class="row result"><div class="col-sm-3 result_headword"><h3 headword_id="16440">dílmain</h3><span class="citation">Cite this: eDIL s.v. dílmain or <a class="reference" href="16440">dil.ie/16440</a></span><br/><span class="revision">Last Revised:

      <span class="pos adj" style="color: #ff8000">adj</span> 
      <span class="itype i">i</span> (dí + loman <span class="ediltr">leash</span>, O'Rahilly, 
        <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/SGS" title="eDIL Bibliography">SGS</strong></span> ii 17 ff
      . <span class="lbl">q.v.</span>).</p>
  
  
    <p class="result">The primary meaning appears to be <span class="trans">
        <span class="def">free</span> from <span class="def">restraint</span> (?), <span class="def">without attachment</span>
      </span>; hence</p>
   

    
      <p class="result">(<span class="edilb">a</span>) applied to actions, <span class="trans">that which is <span class="def">free</span>, <span class="def">permissbile</span>, <span class="def">without hindrance</span> or <span class="def">penalty</span>
        </span>, and</p>

          <span class="def">free</span> (<i>to act</i>), <span class="def">unhampered</span>
        </span>. The connexion is
