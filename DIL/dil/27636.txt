<div class="row result"><div class="col-sm-3 result_headword"><h3 headword_id="27636">2 imm</h3><span class="citation">Cite this: eDIL s.v. 2 imm or <a class="reference" href="27636">dil.ie/27636</a></span><br/><p style="margin-top: 4px" class="small">Forms:
                imm[e]cal</p></div><div class="col-sm-9 result_content">
   


    <p class="result">used as an intensive <span class="pos prefix" style="color: #ff8000">prefix</span>. Rare in O. Ir. Becomes 
very common in Early Mod. Ir. Sometimes appears to 
add little or nothing to the meaning of the word. It is 
used with (<span class="ref"><a class="normal" href="search?search_in=headword&amp;q=2 imm (a)">a</a></span>) adjectives, (<span class="ref"><a class="normal" href="search?search_in=headword&amp;q=2 imm (b)">b</a></span>) nouns, and (<span class="ref"><a class="normal" href="search?search_in=headword&amp;q=2 imm (c)">c</a></span>) verbs.</p>
