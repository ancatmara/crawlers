<div class="row result"><div class="col-sm-3 result_headword"><h3 headword_id="35150">resíu, riasíu</h3><span class="citation">Cite this: eDIL s.v. resíu, riasíu or <a class="reference" href="35150">dil.ie/35150</a></span><br/></div><div class="col-sm-9 result_content">
   
    <p class="result">
      <span class="pos conj." style="color: #ff8000">conj.</span> (prep. ré + <span class="case d">d</span>
      <span class="number s.">s.</span> pron. síu) lit. `before this', 
hence as temp. conj. <span class="trans">
        <span class="def">before</span>
      </span>. In later Ir. re- is dropt.</p>
   
    
      <p class="result"> 
(<span class="edilb">a</span>) in O.Ir. followed by (usually with -ro- or equivalent) 
subj.: molid resíu ro cursacha <span class="trans">
          <span class="ediltr">he praises before he blames</span>
        </span>, 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Wb." title="eDIL Bibliography">Wb.</strong></span> 
4a2
        . dofoídid resíu rís-sa <span class="trans">
          <span class="ediltr">send (him) before I arrive</span>
        </span>, 
          14a17
        . 
robtar irlithi . . . resíu tised hiress <span class="trans">
          <span class="ediltr">before the faith came</span>
        </span>, 

          27c8
        . 
          amal rumboi a n-aesaerge do cholain Crist risiu adcheth 
druailned, 
            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Ml." title="eDIL Bibliography">Ml.</strong></span> 38c9
          
         (lenition of vb. irregular, see 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Thurn." title="eDIL Bibliography">Thurn. Hdb.</strong></span> § 500
        ). 
          dochum . . . inna loc hi ro batar riam cid resiu 
dondichsitis asin doíri, 
            104c5
          
         (rel. -n- irreg.).</p>
    
  
  
    
      <p class="result"> 
(<span class="edilb">b</span>) in Mid.Ir. the subj. mood is used after resiu to express 
an action future or contingent in relation to the vb. of the 
main clause, <span class="lbl">e.g.</span> after an imp. or pres. or fut. tense: resiu tias 
don bith buidhi | beir uaim uili cech n-eccoir <span class="trans">
          <span class="ediltr">before I go</span>
        </span>, 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/&#xC9;riu" title="eDIL Bibliography">Ériu</strong></span> 
iii 31.8
        . 
          marbaid in fer resiu dech uain, 
            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Death-talesoftheUlsterheroes" title="eDIL Bibliography">Death-tales of the Ulster heroes</strong></span> 38 z.
         
tabair usce dún resíu marbai sínd <span class="trans">
          <span class="ediltr">before thou killest us</span>
        </span>, 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/PH" title="eDIL Bibliography">PH</strong></span> 
2333
        . 
          riasiu tīs do ṡaicarbaic | coibsen leire do nertad, 
            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/ZCP" title="eDIL Bibliography">ZCP</strong></span> 
vii 311 § 17
          
        . 
          benaid-sium a chend riasiu sessed do chum 
talman, 
            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/TBC-LL1" title="eDIL Bibliography">TBC-LL¹</strong></span> 1294
          
         (= rainic talmain, St.). 
          rofhitir bar 
n-athair . . . cid riasíu dognéthi a atach, 
            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/PH" title="eDIL Bibliography">PH</strong></span> 7843
          
        . 
          resiu .i. 
inni is taisecha, <span class="frn ">ut est</span> fogella risiu imgona, 
            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/ODav." title="eDIL Bibliography">O'Dav.</strong></span> 1403
          
        . 

          riasíu rister forcend peinne, 
            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/RC" title="eDIL Bibliography">RC</strong></span> xv 488 § 6
          
        . </p>
     
      <p class="result"> 
(The indic. is rarely used in such cases: 
          nom-díultfa-sa 
anocht . . . resíu goires in coilech, 
            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/PH" title="eDIL Bibliography">PH</strong></span> 3196
          
        . marbaid missi 
. . . resiu faicfi me sunda <span class="trans">
          <span class="ediltr">before [the Cyclops] sees me</span>
        </span>, 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Aen." title="eDIL Bibliography">Aen.</strong></span> 149
         
(`<span class="quote">rather than leave me here</span>', Calder)). 
          foceird a bunsuig . . . 
riasiu dofaethsat [<span class="lbl">leg.</span> -sad] for talmain nus-gabed [<span class="lbl">leg.</span> 
-gaibet] na secht milcoin, 
            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/ZCP" title="eDIL Bibliography">ZCP</strong></span> iv 34.8
          
         (TBFr. Eg.) = 
          síu 
cotrí ri talmain, 
            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/LL" title="eDIL Bibliography">LL</strong></span> 248b17
          
         (
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/TBFr." title="eDIL Bibliography">TBFr.</strong></span> 138
        ) = riasiu conrí co 
talmain, YBL (
          <span class="lbl">ib.</span> 16.9
        ).</p>
    
  
  
    
      <p class="result"> 
(<span class="edilb">c</span>) the indic. is used when the action of the follg. vb. is 
already past, the vb. in the main clause being in pret. or perf. 
tense: 
          atchualatar a fherta resíu rosiacht, 
            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/PH" title="eDIL Bibliography">PH</strong></span> 534
          
        . 
          co mba 
marb . . . riasíu ránic a thech, 
            2478
          
        . 
          co riacht a thech resiu 
ro ba matin, 
            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Death-talesoftheUlsterheroes" title="eDIL Bibliography">Death-tales of the Ulster heroes</strong></span> 40.12
          
        . ro thomail C. ní don fleid résiú 
ro beannaighedh hí <span class="trans">
          <span class="ediltr">before it was blessed</span>
        </span>, 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/MR" title="eDIL Bibliography">MR</strong></span> 28.4
        .</p>
     
      <p class="result"> 
The subj. is rare in such cases: nat ro bai talam . . . riasiu 
asrobrath no beitis duile <span class="trans">
          <span class="ediltr">before He said that the elements 
should exist</span>
        </span>, 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/&#xC9;riu" title="eDIL Bibliography">Ériu</strong></span> ii 104 § 15
        . 
          ro bae Eabra . . . la Fenius 
rias[i]u tissad on Scithia, 
            <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/Auraic." title="eDIL Bibliography">Auraic.</strong></span> 28
          
        . ba sai . . . cid siu tisad 
a tuaid <span class="trans">
          <span class="ediltr">even before he came</span>
        </span>, 
          387
         = 
          cidh riesiu, 
            2471
          
         (YBL).</p>
