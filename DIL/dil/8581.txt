    <p class="result"> 
Orig. <span class="trans">on <span class="def">this</span> <span class="def">side</span> of</span> (see 
        <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/GOI" title="eDIL Bibliography">GOI</strong></span> 501

      ), 
hence <span class="trans">
        <span class="def">apart</span> from, <span class="def">besides</span>
      </span> <span class="lbl">etc.</span> (see <span class="edilb">I(a)</span> and <span class="edilb">III</span> below). 
The usual meaning at all stages of the language is <span class="trans">
        <span class="def">without</span>, 
<span class="def">deprived</span> of, <span class="def">not</span> <span class="def">having</span>, -less</span>. Freq. with follg. <span class="pos Vn." style="color: #ff8000">Vn.</span> with which, 
in its verbal function, it serves as neg. particle (see <span class="edilb">II</span>).</p>
