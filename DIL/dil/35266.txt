<div class="row result"><div class="col-sm-3 result_headword"><h3 headword_id="35266">ríg-</h3><span class="citation">Cite this: eDIL s.v. ríg- or <a class="reference" href="35266">dil.ie/35266</a></span><br/><span class="revision">Last Revised:
                2019</span><br/><p style="margin-top: 4px" class="small">Forms:
                righamuis, rídáil, rigdoirsi, rigduini, righfheinnidh, ríghfhlaithe, rígleptha, rígniath</p></div><div class="col-sm-9 result_content">
   
    <p class="result">
      <span class="pos n" style="color: #ff8000">n</span> stem of <span class="ref"><a class="normal" href="search?search_in=headword&amp;q=r&#xED;">rí</a></span> <span class="trans">
        <span class="def">king</span>
      </span>, used to form compds.</p>
   
    
      <p class="result">(<span class="edilb">a</span>) in sense <span class="trans">
          <span class="def">royal</span>, 
pertaining to a <span class="def">king</span> or <span class="def">chief</span>
        </span>, </p>
     
  
    
      <p class="result">(<span class="edilb">b</span>) <span class="trans">
          <span class="def">pre-eminent</span> of its kind; <span class="def">great</span>, 
<span class="def">immense</span>
        </span>, used widely, esp. in poetic style, occas. with adj. 
and npr. Somet. written rí-. Permanent compds. in sense 
(<span class="edilb">a</span>) with technical signification are given separately.</p>
