      <span class="itype &#x101;">ā</span>, <span class="gen f.">f.</span> and róim (Lat. <span class="frn ">Roma</span>) strictly `<span class="quote">the city of Rome</span>' (see 
above), hence used in early Ir. relig. lit. of <span class="trans">a <span class="def">saint's</span> <span class="def">settlement</span>

in róm relgech ná rairged <span class="quote">`the burying ground that was not 
rifled'</span>,
