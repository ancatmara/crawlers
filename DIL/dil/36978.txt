      <span class="itype o">o</span>, <span class="gen m.">m.</span> (AN <span class="frn ">sergent</span>,

      ), <span class="lbl">cf.</span> <span class="ref"><a class="normal" href="search?search_in=headword&amp;q=sarsen">sarsen</a></span>. `seirseanach,

      </span>',

       `seirsenach

      </span>, . . .', O'R. Two exx. support the meaning 
<span class="trans">
        <span class="def">sagittarius</span>
      </span>: 
        Mathghamhuin . . . do mharbad le sersénach do 
muinter Domnaill, 
          <span><strong class="pop" style="color:blue" href="bibliography/ALC" title="eDIL Bibliography">ALC</strong></span> i 196.16
        
      = <span class="frn ">ab aliquo sagitario</span>,
